# Early Revenue stage — 11 to 25 people

> Repeatable processes, customer success, growth engine taking shape.

You're past survival mode. The challenge now is repeatability — things that worked when three people could just talk to each other start breaking when you're fifteen. This is the stage where informal systems need to become documented processes.
