# Scaled stage — 51+ people

> Professional operations, efficiency, continuous improvement.

At this stage all processes should exist in some form. The question is no longer "do we have this?" but "is it optimized, automated, and owned by the right function?" The focus shifts from building processes to improving them.

---

## Critical at this stage

All processes are either critical or recommended. The table below highlights where the most common breakdowns happen at this size.

| Process | Common failure mode at this stage |
|---|---|
| [1.1 Company Planning & Goals](../processes/strategic/1.1--scaled.md) | OKR cascade breaks down, teams lose line of sight to company goals |
| [1.1b Team & Cascading Goals](../processes/strategic/1.1b--scaled.md) | Accountability gaps between department and individual level |
| [1.2 Internal Comms — Strategy](../processes/strategic/1.2--scaled.md) | Town halls become one-way broadcasts, feedback loops close |
| [1.3 Operational Comms Norms](../processes/strategic/1.3--scaled.md) | Communication architecture hasn't scaled with the org |
| [1.4 Who Decides What](../processes/strategic/1.4--scaled.md) | Turf wars or excessive escalation — authority matrix not maintained |
| [1.5 Org Design](../processes/strategic/1.5--scaled.md) | Structure doesn't match how work actually flows |
| [1.6 Data & Reporting Infra](../processes/strategic/1.6--growth.md) | Multiple sources of truth, data team overwhelmed |
| [1.7 Process Documentation](../processes/strategic/1.7--scaled.md) | Docs exist but aren't maintained, no one trusts them |
| [1.8 Cross-Team Coordination](../processes/strategic/1.8--scaled.md) | Too many meetings OR teams ignoring each other |
| [2.1 Cash Flow Management](../processes/financial/2.1--scaled.md) | Treasury and working capital management not yet professionalized |
| [2.2 Financial Planning](../processes/financial/2.2--scaled.md) | Full FP&A cycle, scenario planning, investor reporting package |
| [2.3 Accounts Receivable](../processes/financial/2.3--scaled.md) | DSO not tracked, AR function not integrated with financial systems |
| [3.2 Recruitment & Hiring](../processes/people/3.2--scaled.md) | Employer brand weak, pipeline not built ahead of need |
| [3.5 Performance Management](../processes/people/3.5--scaled.md) | Calibration inconsistent across departments, career frameworks vague |
| [4.1 Legal & Compliance](../processes/legal/4.1--scaled.md) | Compliance becoming a standalone function — SOC 2, GDPR, exit prep |
| [5.1 Sales Operations](../processes/revenue/5.1--scaled.md) | RevOps alignment breaking down between sales, marketing, CS |
| [5.4 Customer Health & Support](../processes/revenue/5.4--scaled.md) | CS team not segmented by tier, health scores not driving action |
| [5.5 Renewal & Retention](../processes/revenue/5.5--scaled.md) | Churn not reported to board, win-back campaigns missing |

## Key milestone at this stage

Self-sustaining operational excellence. Systems run without founder involvement. Continuous improvement is cultural, not a project.
