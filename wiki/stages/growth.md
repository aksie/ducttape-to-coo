# Growth stage — 26 to 50 people

> Scale systems, departmental structure, don't break as you stretch.

This is the stage where everything that was informal either gets formalized or causes a crisis. You now have departments, managers, and enough complexity that the founder can't hold it all. The processes listed here are the ones that break most visibly at this size.

---
