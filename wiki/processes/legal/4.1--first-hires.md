# Legal (above certain size also Compliance) — First Hires

> <!-- One sentence: what this process is and why it matters specifically at this stage. -->

## What good looks like at this stage

Every new hire and contractor needs a signed contract with an IP assignment clause before they start. Don't DIY this — use standard templates your lawyer has approved once. If you're handling any personal data (user or employee), put a basic privacy policy in place. Don't let legal slip into 'we'll sort it later'.
