# Legal (above certain size also Compliance) — Growth

> <!-- One sentence: what this process is and why it matters specifically at this stage. -->

## What good looks like at this stage

Legal volume is growing faster than you expect. Put a legal ops rhythm in place: contract register, external counsel briefed and on retainer, clear authority matrix for who can sign what. GDPR/privacy compliance should be properly documented now, not just theoretical. Compliance requirements (SOC 2, ISO, sector-specific) need an owner.
