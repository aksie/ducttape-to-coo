# Legal (above certain size also Compliance) — Foundation

> <!-- One sentence: what this process is and why it matters specifically at this stage. -->

## What good looks like at this stage

Get the company structure right from day one. Sign a co-founder agreement that covers equity splits, vesting, and what happens if someone leaves. Have every founder assign their IP to the company — this will come up in every future due diligence. Keep it simple: use standard templates and a startup-savvy lawyer, not a corporate firm.
