# Onboarding — First Hires

> <!-- One sentence: what this process is and why it matters specifically at this stage. -->

## What good looks like at this stage

Make a simple checklist: laptop ordered, accounts set up, contracts signed before day one. On day one: walk them through what you're building, who does what, and how you work. A bad first week is hard to recover from. Campground rule: every new hire should leave the onboarding docs better than they found them.
