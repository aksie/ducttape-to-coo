# Shareholder & Investor Reporting — First Hires

> Written reporting is for **you** first — external investors are an audience layer on a habit that keeps you honest. Start monthly before you have shareholders; widen distribution when angels arrive.

## What good looks like at this stage

- **A monthly written update runs on calendar cadence.**
  Even pre-funding — recipients can be only you and the leadership team. Use the same structure you will send investors later. This helps you honestly evaluate progress in the founding team, not just report upward.
  <!-- sources: src-058 (practitioner contribution, human:stefanverkerk) -->

- **Each update scores last period's goals in writing.**
  Tie lookback to your [quarterly goals](../../../templates/docs/quarterly-goals.md) doc or the goals you stated in the prior update — with honest notes on what went differently than expected.
  <!-- sources: src-058 (practitioner contribution, human:stefanverkerk), src-059 (template synthesis) -->

## What you actually need to do

1. **Start now — send the first edition to yourself or co-founders.**
   Use the [investor update template](../../../templates/rendered/investor-update.html): highlights, **misses/help up front**, checkbox lookback, checkbox lookahead (product / growth / finance). Quarterly runway and scenario sections can wait.
   <!-- sources: src-058 (human:stefanverkerk), src-059, templates/docs/investor-update.md -->

2. **On lookback, explain the delta — not just checkboxes.**
   For each goal: what you did, what outcome differed from plan, what you learned. Performing confidence is not the point; documenting reality is.
   <!-- sources: src-058 (human:stefanverkerk) -->

3. **When angels or F&F investors are on the cap table, add them to the same email.**
   Do not invent a new format under fundraising pressure. Keep monthly short form; add quarterly depth (runway, burn, scenarios) once per quarter.
   <!-- sources: src-058 (human:stefanverkerk), src-059, processes.json 1.2b stageFocus -->

4. **Name explicit asks when you need help.**
   Warm intro, customer lead, advisor — with a pasteable blurb investors can forward. Updates that only report good news train investors to ignore you when you actually need something.
   <!-- sources: src-059 (template synthesis) -->

## Warning signs you're behind

### Founder / key-person time

- **Investor alignment happens only in coffees and calls.**
  You have said "ships in six weeks" three times without a written record of the slip. Verbal optimism without lookback lets you kid yourself.
  <!-- sources: src-058 (human:stefanverkerk) -->

### Process entry

- **You cannot point to last month's stated goals and how you scored against them.**
  Highlights-only emails are a newsletter, not a reporting process.
  <!-- sources: src-059 (template synthesis) -->

## How this evolves next

At **early revenue**, reporting adds board prep (deck, pre-read), cap table hygiene, and coordination with legal on shareholder matters — the monthly email habit continues; governance layers on top.
<!-- sources: processes.json 1.2b stageFocus early-revenue -->

## Context variants

### If you are bootstrapped (no external investors yet)

- **Audience is internal only — that is correct.**
  Leadership team or founder-only distribution still counts. The discipline is writing the lookback; investors are not the reason to start.
  <!-- sources: src-058 (human:stefanverkerk) -->

### If you have angel or friends & family investors

- **Same monthly template, wider BCC.**
  Key metrics, progress against quarterly goals, what you need help with — keep it simple.
  <!-- sources: processes.json 1.2b stageFocus, src-058 (human:stefanverkerk) -->

### If you are venture-backed (seed / Series A)

- **Monthly investor email plus quarterly board meeting.**
  Five-to-ten slide deck, pre-read 48 hours ahead, written board minutes. Cap table in Carta, Pulley, or Ledgy from day one of the round. Information rights in the term sheet usually require this cadence.
  <!-- sources: processes.json 1.2b variants venture-early -->
