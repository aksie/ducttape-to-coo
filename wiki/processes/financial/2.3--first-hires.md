# Accounts Receivable (Outgoing Invoices) — First Hires

> <!-- One sentence: what this process is and why it matters specifically at this stage. -->

## What good looks like at this stage

Invoice your first customers as soon as the deal is signed. Track payment status in a spreadsheet. Follow up personally if they're late — it's normal to chase at this stage. Build relations with finance ops people at customers so cash doesn't get stuck for admin reasons.
