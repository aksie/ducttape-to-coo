# Financial Administration — Foundation

> At foundation, formal financial administration means one thing: don't do it yourself. Outsource bookkeeping from day one and build the habit of capturing every receipt and invoice in a known place.

## What you actually need to do

1. **Create a `/finance/` folder in your company filing structure before transactions pile up.** Even at foundation you will have invoices, bank statements, and tax correspondence. One known home beats searching email when you need something later.
   <!-- sources: src-039 (Practitioner contribution — Formal Financial Filing) -->

2. **Set up a basic chart of accounts and agree an automation path with your bookkeeper.** Choose accounting software (Moneybird, Exact, Xero, QuickBooks, or equivalent) and confirm how data will flow — API, bank feed, or structured export. Migrations are painful; starting right is cheap.
   <!-- sources: src-039 (Practitioner contribution — Formal Financial Filing) -->

## How this evolves next

- **At First Hires, payroll tax filings join the picture and month-end close becomes a rhythm.** Your external bookkeeper should run close; you feed clean data: receipts in, invoices out, payroll records on time.
