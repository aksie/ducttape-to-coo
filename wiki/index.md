# Duct Tape to COO — Wiki

Operational guidance for startup founders and operators, organized by growth stage.

## How to navigate

**If you want to know what matters right now:** go to your stage portal below.  
**If you want to understand a specific process in depth:** go directly to the process page.  
**If you're an agent or contributor:** see the [template](processes/_template.md) and the [content map](#content-map) below.
