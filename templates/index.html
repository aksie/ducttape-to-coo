External resources — Netherlands legal templates

Complementary to the ops structures above: where files live vs what contracts say. Wiki: 4.1 Governance, 4.5 Internal & Labour, 4.6 Privacy & IP.

Capital Waters — open-source investment documents (governance & fundraising).

  • Cap table (Excel) — /01-corporate/shareholders/
  • Ordinary share subscription & shareholders agreement — co-founder or friends-and-family equity
  • Convertible loan (CLA) or EPOS (Dutch SAFE) — angel money before a priced round
  • Preferred share subscription, shareholders agreement & term sheet — seed / Series A

Capital Waters: CC BY-ND 4.0 (“comply or explain”: show deviations, keep the Capital Waters header). Netherlands only — get counsel before signing.

Penrose — free Word/PDF model contracts (corporate, employment, privacy, commercial starters). English versions.

  • Bestuursbesluit, aandeelhoudersbesluit, AVA-oproeping, aandeelhoudersovereenkomst — /01-corporate/ (pick one SHA basis with Capital Waters if you have investors)
  • Arbeidsovereenkomst (onbepaald / bepaald), VSO — /06-hr/
  • Verwerkersovereenkomst (DPA), NDA — /07-compliance/gdpr/, /03-contracts/ndas/
  • LOI, SPA, APA — /08-transaction-prep/ (deal active only)

Penrose: no open licence; templates are starting points only — not legal advice. Get counsel before signing.
