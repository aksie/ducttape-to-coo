# 📋 Due diligence checklist — [Transaction / round]

Master checklist of what buyers and investors typically request, with expected format and where to store each item. **Sections 01–07** mirror the [Company Ops Registry](../rendered/company-ops-registry.html). **Section 08** is deal-specific depth — populate only when fundraising or M&A is live.

Each line item has an **Owner** (who is gathering or validating it) and a **Status** checkbox with % done. Copy into Notion or Sheets, [copy the markdown](../docs/due-diligence.md), or [Edit source on GitHub](https://github.com/aksie/ducttape-to-coo/edit/main/templates/docs/due-diligence.md) to fork or copy the markdown into your own repo.
