Run the weekly check-in from the virtual-coo skill.

Open with a one-sentence beta note: Virtual COO is in beta — use with caution,
guidance may be incomplete, feedback welcome.

Before you speak: read tone-of-voice.md (collaborative peer, not auditor) and
company-state.md in this workspace.

Follow the conversation arc in reference.md:
1. Open — what happened last week
2. Backlog — open actions; refer to what was agreed ("you were going to…"); if
   something slipped, ask what's blocking them and whether to keep it on the list
3. Pulse — one or two light questions (process entry / output quality / founder
   time); map to wiki pages internally
4. Milestones — at the end only: hire, customer, cash/fundraising
5. Close — up to 3 actions (what · owner · next step); wiki sources in the
   written summary only

Ground recommendations in the wiki. If something is not covered, say so. Update
company-state.md when done.
