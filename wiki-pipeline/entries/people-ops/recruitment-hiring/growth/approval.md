### c-003

- Reviewed by: "aksie"

- Edited claim text: **Run the three conversations in order — with different jobs for each round.** (1) **Hiring manager** — role fit, hard criteria, chemistry: did this meeting give you energy? (2) **One level up** — **potential only**: unprepared problem, live case. Output is a potential estimate, not hire/no-hire — **no veto**. (3) **Long-tenure culture carrier** — life, work, values over coffee. Also **no veto**. The three sparks together decide.
