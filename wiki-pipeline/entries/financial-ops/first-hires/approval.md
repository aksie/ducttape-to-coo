- Edited claim text: Move all business expenses onto one or a small set of dedicated cards. Even smoother for the company is when founders / employees pay expenses (anything except SAAS stuff) on their personal card, and only get reimbursed if they have the receipt. 

Because the cost of admin from a $20 coffee recept with a potential customer is much higher than $7. The rule is that no personal expenses mix in. Because at founder marginal tax rates (50%+), every missed deductible expense costs roughly half its value.
