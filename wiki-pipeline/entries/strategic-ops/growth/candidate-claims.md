## Candidate claims from GitLab Company-Level Planning & Goals

### c-gitlab-planning-growth-001: Planning becomes a cadence system
- Section: what_good_looks_like
- Supporting atoms: atom-080-gitlab
- Candidate claim: At growth stage, planning should become a cadence system across time horizons: week, month, quarter, year, strategy, vision, and mission.
- Synthesis notes: GitLab's 2019 CEO cadence page is the bridge from goals/OKRs to an operating rhythm.

### c-gitlab-planning-growth-002: Put OKRs inside the operating calendar
- Section: evolution
- Supporting atoms: atom-081-gitlab
- Candidate claim: The next maturity step after cascading OKRs is to place OKRs inside a broader operating calendar with board meetings, sales targets, metrics reviews, yearly planning, strategy, vision, and mission.
- Synthesis notes: Useful longitudinal lesson: OKRs are necessary but not enough once recurring company rituals multiply.
