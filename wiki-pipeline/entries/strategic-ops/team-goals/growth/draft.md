# Team Goals & Cascading Goals — Growth (~50–150 people)

> At growth stage, company goals cascade up reliably before they cascade down. Team goals should mirror the company split between running metrics and locally owned change goals — with slack for dependencies baked in.

## What good looks like at this phase

<!-- claim-id: c-001 -->
- **Team-level planning mirrors the company split: running metrics from C-level, change goals owned locally, with ~70% of departmental capacity pre-booked on local objectives.** Cascade goals fail when departments inherit top-line metrics without local problem ownership.
  <!-- sources: src-064 (anonymous-practitioner) -->

<!-- claim-id: c-002 -->
- **Cross-team dependency slack is built into departmental plans — not added as an afterthought.** Dependencies surface at scale; slack reserved upfront prevents gridlock when marketing, engineering, and legal need each other mid-quarter.
  <!-- sources: src-064 (anonymous-practitioner) -->
