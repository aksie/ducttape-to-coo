- Edited claim text: **[NL] Set up hour registration from your first employee — this is a legal obligation, not optional.** Dutch law (Arbeidstijdenwet) requires objective, reliable, and accessible time records for all employees including remote and flexible workers. Name the tool, who approves timesheets, and the submission deadline (e.g. weekly by Friday) in your handbook. Records must be kept for at least one year and employees must have access to their own.

- Edited claim text: **[NL] Write down your sick leave notification procedure before anyone gets sick.** Dutch law (WVP) requires this in writing. Document: who the employee reports to and by what time on day one, what happens at the key milestones (day 6, week 6, week 42), and the role of the company doctor (bedrijfsarts). This is the employer's re-integration roadmap — missing a milestone can extend the two-year pay obligation by another year.
