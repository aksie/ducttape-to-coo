# Privacy & IP — First Hires (2–10 people)

> Two early baselines: know whether your value rests on proprietary IP, and know what personal data you process — with reviewed policies and processor agreements in place.

## What good looks like at this phase

<!-- claim-id: c-001 -->
- **You know whether your value and competitive position rest on proprietary IP.** Brand, design, software, invention, trade secret — that answer determines how much of the rest of the IP work matters for you, so IP effort stays proportional to how much your edge actually comes from it.
  <!-- sources: src-061 (practitioner, Trailhead legal) -->

<!-- claim-id: c-002 -->
- **You know what personal data you process, your policies are reviewed, and processors are under agreement.** You can say whether you handle sensitive data (health, financial, biometric); your privacy and cookie policies are up to date, public and legally reviewed; and you have a data processing agreement with every third party that processes personal data on your behalf.
  <!-- sources: src-061 (practitioner, Trailhead legal) -->
