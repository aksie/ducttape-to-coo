- Flags: []
- Reviewer notes: ""
- Reviewed by: "aksie"
- Reviewed date: 2026-06-03 17:47
- Edited claim text: **Start tracking working hours for every employee from their first day but do it extremely simple and pragmatic** Dutch law (Arbeidstijdenwet) requires objective, reliable, and accessible time records for all employees, regardless of role or arrangement. Records must be kept for at least one year. This is not optional and not staged by headcount — it applies from the moment the first employment contract is signed. But again, do this very pragmatically.
