## Warning signs you're behind

### Output quality

<!-- claim-id: c-001 -->
- **The template was updated correctly, but the salary on file no longer matches what's actually paid.** A term gets fixed (e.g. 7 months → a year) while pay drifts away from the record. When documented terms diverge from reality, payroll, disputes, and DD all run against the wrong numbers — at scale, move to an HRIS with a file per employee.
