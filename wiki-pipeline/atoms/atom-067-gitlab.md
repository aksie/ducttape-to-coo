## Claim
At the earliest stage, your expense policy can be a principle instead of a rulebook: spend company money as carefully as your own, but do not block useful work on pre-approval.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab tells team members to spend company money like their own and says they do not need permission before purchases made in the interest of the company.

## Why
When the team is small, trust and speed matter more than formal control. A simple principle lets people buy what they need without turning founders or managers into approval bottlenecks.
