## Claim
At Growth stage, financial operations shifts from founder-managed or outsourced to a dedicated internal finance function — the trigger is usually 30-40 people or Series A close.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Getexact describes the transition to an internal finance hire as typically happening at Series A close or when the company reaches 30-40 people, whichever comes first.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Below this threshold, fractional or outsourced finance is typically more cost-effective. Above it, the coordination overhead and response time of outsourced finance starts to cost more than a full-time hire.
