## Claim

If your only investor communication is verbal — coffees, calls, hallway updates — you can repeat the same "goes live in six weeks" line for twelve weeks without ever recording the slip.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner describes telling the same investor across multiple coffees that a feature ships in six weeks while the timeline keeps moving — never confronted because nothing is written.

## Why

Written lookback against stated goals makes optimism expensive. Verbal updates feel productive but do not create accountability to past-you.
