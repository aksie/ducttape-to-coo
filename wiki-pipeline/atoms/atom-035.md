## Claim
Write down 3-5 company objectives each quarter with measurable key results for each — not a list of tasks, but outcomes you can score at the end of the quarter.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab's OKR guidance: key results must be measurable and gradable. Objectives should be inspirational; key results should be operational. OKRs are not a to-do list.

## Why (if explicit in source)
The difference between a goal and a task is measurability. "Improve customer satisfaction" is a wish. "Reduce average support response time from 48h to 24h" is a key result you can score.
