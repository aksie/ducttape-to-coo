## Claim
Founders and senior leaders should personally deliver company values and origin story during onboarding — not delegate it to HR or a junior team member.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Gilboa: "We think it's vital for the most senior people at the company to be involved in this process, including me and my co-founder. When company values are articulated, explained in detail and reinforced (ideally with examples) by a founder, they carry far more weight than they might when simply written on a piece of paper or a poster on the wall."

## Why (if explicit in source)
Founders are the source of institutional knowledge — origin story, values, why decisions were made. When a junior person delivers this, the message is that it's not actually that important. When a founder delivers it, the message is the opposite.
