## Claim

At early revenue, your offboarding checklist should expand to cover formal HR paperwork, client handover communications, and a habit of feeding exit interview themes back into hiring and management.

## Source quote or paraphrase

OpenOrg adds client notification, compliance documentation, and quarterly aggregation of exit data for leadership; processes.json early-revenue stageFocus requires exit insights to inform how you hire and manage.

## Why

First hires offboarding is survival mode — checklist, revoke, learn. Early revenue adds revenue protection (client continuity) and closes the loop from departure data to retention fixes.
