## Claim

Treating adoption as "filing Thursday, assume approval unless I hear otherwise" is a red flag — late or incorrect filing of annual accounts can trigger director liability.

## Source quote or paraphrase

A director emailing co-shareholders "accounts ready, filing Thursday, assume approval" is not valid adoption; late/incorrect filing → director liability.

## Why

A trustee in an insolvency looks back at filings; sloppy adoption and late filing are exactly the hooks for personal director liability.
