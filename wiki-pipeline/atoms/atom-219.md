## Claim

Anyone who signs employment or commercial contracts on the company's behalf has documented authority and clear boundaries — signing is not assumed from job title alone.

## Source quote or paraphrase

As HR and operational commercial contract work moves from CEO/founder to team leads, don't let them sign without a legal right to do so, and don't let them sign everything they want. Have a lightweight process where you agree in what cases the Ops lead can sign labour contracts, and set the legal framework (power of attorney from formal director/CEO) so contracts are legally valid.

## Why

Delegation without documented authority creates invalid or unenforceable contracts. Delegation without boundaries creates commitments the company never intended. Both problems appear the moment signing moves off the founder's desk.
