## Claim

Set up a minimal legal filing structure from day one: a deed of incorporation, a current shareholder register, and a single known location for corporate documents. At this stage, that is all that's needed — but it needs to exist.

## Source quote or paraphrase

At the foundation stage, good looks like: deed of incorporation filed, shareholder register up to date, and a single folder where corporate documents live. That's it.

## Why

When a company needs to respond to a data room request — whether months or years later — the effort required is proportional to how much work was skipped at the start. Founding documents that live in email or on one person's laptop have a way of disappearing. Starting with a known structure costs almost nothing and prevents scrambling later.
