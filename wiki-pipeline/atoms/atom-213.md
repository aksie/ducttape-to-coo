## Claim

You should explicitly tell the team they do not need to read every Slack message — only channels and @-mentions relevant to their role.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN: "Slack Is Great But — teach people they don't need to read everything; great engineers don't and shouldn't."

## Why

Without permission to ignore noise, people either burn out trying to keep up or miss important @-mentions in the flood.
