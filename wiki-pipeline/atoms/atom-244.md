## Claim

At growth stage, IT access deprovisioning should be triggered on day one of notice — not only on last day — with high-risk credentials revoked immediately regardless of notice period length.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN European and US practitioners distinguish good-faith voluntary partings (weeks of handover) from high-risk unilateral access (immediate kill); processes.json growth stageFocus specifies IT deprovisioning triggered day 1 of notice.

## Why

Larger teams mean more sensitive systems and more departures. Waiting until last day at 30+ people recreates the first-hires failure mode at scale.
