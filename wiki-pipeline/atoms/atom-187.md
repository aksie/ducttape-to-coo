## Claim

Friends-and-family money, hires, suppliers, customers, or contractors operate on handshakes with no signed agreements when real money and obligations are involved.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Nothing on paper when it matters — FFF money, hires, suppliers, customers on vibes; management agreements missing years in.

## Why

Informal deals work until they do not; the cost of fixing missing contracts rises with company value and number of parties involved.
