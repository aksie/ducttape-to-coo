## Claim

At Early Revenue, commercial contract signing authority extends beyond HR — customer MSAs, vendor agreements, and partnership deals need the same delegation discipline.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Operational commercial contract work also moves from CEO/founder to team leads as the company grows — the same signing-authority problem applies beyond labour contracts.

## Why

First hires establishes delegation for employment contracts; early revenue multiplies external agreements. Extending the contract-type matrix before commercial volume grows prevents the same authority and boundary gaps from repeating.
