## Claim

No job opening goes public until budget is confirmed and management agrees on the role — with an indicative salary range by level (junior, senior, manager, VP, executive).

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner: before posting, check budget and get management agreement that this is the job opening you will spend budget on, including indicative salary band by seniority level.

## Why

Unapproved posts commit spend and set candidate expectations before leadership has aligned on headcount and compensation — fixes are slow and relationship-damaging once candidates are in process.
