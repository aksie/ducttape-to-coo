## Claim

When you email a formal update, you should link to the authoritative doc and name where questions or discussion should happen (usually a specific Slack channel).

## Source quote or paraphrase

Buteau: email can announce or summarize, but should link to the durable source and name where Q&A happens.

## Why

Email-only announcements spawn parallel Slack threads with no canonical version; linking prevents two conflicting truths.
