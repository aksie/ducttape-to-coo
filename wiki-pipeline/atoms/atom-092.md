**After each quarter, write a frank analysis of why things went differently than planned and what your priorities are for next quarter.** Send it to someone, even if it's just yourself. This prevents you from saying things like "this feature will launch in 8 weeks" for four months in a row. When priorities are only verbal, it's easy to lose track of what you committed to.
