## Claim

You should use Slack for same-team, time-sensitive coordination and use email or a scheduled call for cross-department or formal whole-team messages.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN: if communication crosses department boundaries or must be formally communicated to an entire team, direct it to email or a scheduled call rather than Slack.

## Why

Slack in large cross-functional channels creates synchronous pressure and notification storms; email and scheduled calls signal deliberation and give recipients batching room.
