## Claim

You should start knowledge transfer in the first days of the notice period — not compress handover, documentation, and access audit into the employee's last day.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Tinyteam checklist phases work across two weeks: week 1 maps responsibilities and schedules transfer sessions; last day is revoke access and recover devices only.

## Why

Small teams lose disproportionate tacit knowledge per departure because roles are broad and documentation is thin. Starting early is the only way to capture what only that person knows before they mentally check out.
