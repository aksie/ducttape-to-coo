## Claim
Track expenses from day one, even if it's just a log of transactions in a spreadsheet.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Zeni argues that retrospectively organizing expenses for past months or years gets disproportionately harder as the business grows, so investing time in expense tracking from the start pays off.

## Why
Memory of transaction context decays fast — you'll remember why you spent $400 at a vendor this week but not nine months from now at tax prep. Reconstructing that context later requires bank statement archaeology and can cost you legitimate deductions.
