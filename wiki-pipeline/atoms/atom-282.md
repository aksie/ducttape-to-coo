## Claim

Store any side or different terms as addenda filed alongside the employment contracts, so a due diligence reconciles — e.g. the salary report shows €90k but the contracts only sum to €75k unless the side letters are on file.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Store side/different terms as addenda alongside the contracts so DD reconciles (report says €90k, contracts sum to €75k without the addenda).

## Why

Unfiled side deals make headcount cost impossible to tie back to signed paper, which reads as a controls gap in due diligence.
