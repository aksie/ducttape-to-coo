## Claim
Get a bookkeeper earlier than you think you need one — they will pay for themselves within 3 months in founder time saved, and you should be focused on building the company not doing data entry.

## Source quote or paraphrase
A practitioner who ran finance at a startup describes getting a bookkeeper earlier than you think as paying for itself within 3 months in founder time saved.

## Why
Every hour the founder spends categorising transactions, reconciling accounts, or chasing receipts is an hour not spent on product, sales, or hiring. At the first-hires stage, founder time is the scarcest resource. A bookkeeper is relatively cheap insurance and frees you up for work only you can do.
