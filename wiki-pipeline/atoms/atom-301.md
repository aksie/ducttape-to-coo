## Claim

Equip the business to handle much of the routine legal itself with good checklists — e.g. standard NDAs don't need an expensive lawyer (unless it's an M&A NDA) — so the first legal hire stays free to zoom out.

## Source quote or paraphrase

A lot of legal day-to-day can be done by the business with a good checklist (standard NDAs don't need a lawyer — unless M&A NDA).

## Why

Routing every routine document through one person is what drowns the first hire; checklists keep the simple work in the business and protect strategic time.
