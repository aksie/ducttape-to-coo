## Claim

Register trademarks and domain names early, and record their renewal dates in a compliance calendar or spreadsheet from the moment you register them.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Register trademarks and domains early; note renewal dates somewhere.

## Why

Trademark and domain registrations lapse silently — there is no urgent reminder, and missing a renewal can mean losing a name you've built a brand on. The effort to record renewal dates at registration time is trivial; the cost of discovering a lapse after the fact is not.
