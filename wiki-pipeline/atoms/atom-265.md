## Claim

CFO or CEO discovers weeks later that a new hire is working but not on payroll or insured because the signed contract never reached HR.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner failure: not on payroll or insured until remediated after contract bypassed HR/CFO/COO.

## Why

Retroactive payroll and insurance fixes consume finance and founder time under urgency — and signal weak controls to investors reviewing HR and legal ops.
