## Claim

You should maintain a master list of every tool, SaaS account, and system each employee can access — updated when you onboard someone new — so offboarding does not depend on remembering services ad hoc.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN security checklist thread recommends documenting every business service, who has admin rights, and paired onboarding/offboarding checklists as baseline startup security hygiene.

## Why

At first hires you add tools quickly and informally. Without an inventory, revocation misses the one account that still has production keys — the failure mode in multiple HN cautionary tales.
