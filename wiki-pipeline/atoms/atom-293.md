## Claim

Copy-pasting someone else's privacy terms is not enough — without internal measures so not everyone can access all data, inadequate protection risks an Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens (AP) fine plus reputational damage, weighted by how sensitive your data is.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Copied terms aren't enough; you need internal access controls; inadequate protection risks an AP fine + reputational damage, depending on data sensitivity.

## Why

Policies on paper don't protect data; without real access controls a breach becomes a fine and a public reputational hit, especially for sensitive data.
