## Claim

At early revenue, you should expand a one-page norms doc into a communication playbook: channel list with jobs, escalation paths, what moves to docs, and which channel is the incident fast lane.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Buteau: define what moves from chat to source of truth, which channel is the fast lane for incidents, and where discussion vs authority live.

## Why

Cross-team handoffs (e.g. CS → engineering) multiply at early revenue; without named escalation paths, urgent issues land in random DMs.
