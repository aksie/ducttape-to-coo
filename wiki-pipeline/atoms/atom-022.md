## Claim
Hire a bookkeeper earlier than you think you need one — the founder time saved within the first 3 months typically exceeds the cost.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Reddit practitioner reports that getting a bookkeeper earlier than felt necessary paid for itself within 3 months in founder time saved, and improved data quality significantly.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Finance admin done by founders is done slowly, reluctantly, and irregularly. A bookkeeper does it faster, more consistently, and frees founder time for higher-leverage work.
