## Claim

Document parental and partner leave entitlements explicitly — Dutch law changed significantly in 2022 and many employees (and founders) don't know what they're entitled to.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Since August 2022: parental leave is 9 weeks at 70% salary via UWV (for children born after that date). Partner leave: 5 days fully paid + up to 5 weeks additional at 70% via UWV. Pregnancy/maternity leave: 16 weeks. These are legal entitlements, not discretionary company benefits.

## Why (inferred from source if not explicit)

The 2022 changes shifted parental leave from fully unpaid to partially paid via UWV — a significant change that many early-stage founders have not updated their understanding for. An employee who doesn't know they're entitled to paid parental leave may not take it, which is a welfare and retention risk. An employer who doesn't know may not submit the UWV claim correctly.
