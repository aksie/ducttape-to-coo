## Claim
Run a monthly P&L review with the founding team — this single habit improves financial decision-making quality more than any tool or process change.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Reddit practitioner describes the monthly P&L review with the founding team as the highest-impact finance change they made. It forced shared understanding of the numbers and stopped decisions being made in isolation.

## Why (if explicit in source)
When founders don't look at the P&L together regularly, they develop different mental models of the company's financial position. Decisions that look rational in isolation create inconsistencies at the company level.
