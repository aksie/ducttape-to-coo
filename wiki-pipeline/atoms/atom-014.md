## Claim
Cash-on-hand and months of runway should be visible to the full leadership team, not just the CEO — this is a sign of financial maturity, not a risk.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Visible.vc argues that restricting runway visibility to the CEO creates a single point of failure in financial decision-making and prevents department heads from making informed tradeoffs.

## Why (if explicit in source)
When only the CEO knows the runway, every spending decision requires escalation. Sharing the number enables distributed decision-making and signals trust in the leadership team.
