## Claim
Your chart of accounts should be set up to reflect your business model from the start — restructuring it later is disruptive and makes historical comparisons unreliable.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Bench argues that the chart of accounts is foundational — wrong categories set early propagate through all historical reporting and are painful to correct without restatements.

## Why (if explicit in source)
The chart of accounts determines what you can and cannot see in your P&L. A chart designed for a different business model creates blind spots that persist until a full restructuring.
