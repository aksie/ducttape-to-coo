## Claim
If your goal list has more than 5 items, you don't have priorities — you have a backlog dressed up as a plan. Cut until it hurts.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab's OKR guidance: "OKRs are not a laundry list — if everything is a priority, nothing is." The value of the exercise is in the cutting, not the listing.

## Why (if explicit in source)
A long list of goals signals that you haven't made hard tradeoffs yet. The planning process should force decisions about what not to do, not just what to do.
