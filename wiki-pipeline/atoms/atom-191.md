## Claim

Escalate legal documentation in step with real risk — traction, people, money, data — not with founder anxiety alone.

## Source quote or paraphrase

The trick is to know which phase you are in and escalate paperwork in step with real risk, not with anxiety.

## Why

Both over- and under-legalising fail for the same reason: misjudging which risks are live now versus later; phasing keeps cost and protection aligned.
