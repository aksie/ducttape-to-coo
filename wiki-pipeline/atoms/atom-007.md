## Claim
If your expense categories have been applied inconsistently, your historical data is unreliable for forecasting — this is more common than founders realise and the fix is painful.

## Source quote or paraphrase
CFO Dive sources describe inconsistent expense categorisation as one of the most common and damaging finance mistakes, often discovered only when a new finance hire or auditor reviews the books.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Forecasting requires pattern recognition in historical data. If the same type of expense has been coded to three different categories over 18 months, there is no usable pattern to forecast from.
