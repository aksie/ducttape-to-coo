## Claim

Put a trigger in place so contracts that deviate from standard — above a monetary threshold and/or of strategic value — get legal review; use change-of-control clauses as the worked example, since the value is knowing which important contracts contain one and signing consciously.

## Source quote or paraphrase

A trigger so non-standard contracts (over a threshold and/or strategic) get reviewed; change-of-control is the worked example — value is knowing which contracts contain it and deciding consciously.

## Why

You often cannot renegotiate a change-of-control clause, but knowing where they sit lets you avoid nasty surprises in an acquisition or financing.
