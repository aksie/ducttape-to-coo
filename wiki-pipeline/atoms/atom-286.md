## Claim

Material client contracts run off lawyer-reviewed templates, and material commercial contracts (suppliers, leases, IT service providers) are reviewed by counsel before signing — not patched up afterwards.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Material client contracts on lawyer-reviewed templates; material commercial contracts reviewed by counsel before signing, not patched afterwards.

## Why

Fixing terms after signature means renegotiating from a weak position; review before signing is where you still have leverage.
