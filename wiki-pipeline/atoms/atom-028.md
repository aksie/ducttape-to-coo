## Claim
Goals should be set at least 60% bottom-up by the team — not handed down entirely by founders. People execute better on goals they helped define.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Davis: "You want 60% of your company's OKRs to be defined by employees, not leadership. You don't want the executives coming in dictating what all the goals are going to be."

## Why (if explicit in source)
Bottom-up goals surface real priorities from people closest to the work. They also create ownership — people are more motivated to hit goals they proposed than goals handed to them.
