## Claim

Block an evening with your co-founder to agree scenarios — roles, money in, exit triggers — and explicitly commit to formalising entity, IP, and founder terms once traction is real.

Sit down for an evening, agree scenarios, and say: once we see this is real, we will formalise.

## Why

A agreed trigger for formalisation prevents drifting for years on verbal deals and makes the incorporation step deliberate rather than reactive panic.
