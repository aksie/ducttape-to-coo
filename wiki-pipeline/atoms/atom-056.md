## Claim
An AP backlog is invisible until it causes a payment crisis — keep a simple tracker of what invoices are owed and when they are due so you never miss a payment.

## Source quote or paraphrase
A practitioner who ran finance at a 15-25 person startup describes AP backlogs as invisible until they cause a payment crisis. A simple tracker prevents this entirely.

## Why
When invoices pile up untracked, you don't discover the problem until a vendor calls about a missed payment, a service gets cut off, or a relationship is damaged. At the first-hires stage, you are small enough that every missed payment is noticeable and reputationally costly. A simple tracker gives you visibility before it becomes a crisis.
