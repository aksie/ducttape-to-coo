## Claim
A simple shared spreadsheet reviewed weekly by the founding team — with current cash, projected cash in 4 and 13 weeks, and key assumptions listed — is sufficient for cash visibility at early scale.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Several HN practitioners describe this low-tech approach as more effective than elaborate models because the simplicity means it actually gets updated and reviewed consistently.

## Why (if explicit in source)
The best cash forecast is the one that gets used. Elaborate models that require 2 hours to update get skipped; a simple spreadsheet that takes 15 minutes gets done every week.
