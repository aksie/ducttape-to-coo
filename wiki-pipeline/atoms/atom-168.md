## Claim

Once you give away equity to an external investor, expect to professionalise founder documentation, cap table hygiene, and IP ownership beyond co-founder handshakes.

## Source quote or paraphrase

The moment you give away a piece of what two founders built, you need more than a handshake. A third party with real money will expect professionalisation.

## Why

External capital introduces accountability and future rounds; undocumented internals that angels tolerated will not survive institutional diligence.
