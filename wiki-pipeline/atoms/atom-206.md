## Claim

You should acknowledge messages quickly with a lightweight signal (emoji reaction or short "ack") so senders know you saw it without requiring a full reply yet.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Vohra's Acknowledgment Principle: recipients send acknowledgment as fast as reasonably possible — emoji reaction is a valid form.

## Why

Async comms lack non-verbal cues; without acks, senders chase or duplicate messages, doubling noise.
