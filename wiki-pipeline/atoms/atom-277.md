## Claim

Map the obligations that flow from any financing: read the loan terms for what must be reported and when (notification duties, covenants that trigger if you drop below certain values), and put the recurring reports in the calendar — finance owns them, but legal should know what's in there.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Obligations flow from financing — bank reporting duties and covenants; check loan terms for required reports and timing, put recurring reports in the calendar (finance's responsibility).

## Why

Covenant and reporting breaches are avoidable but bite hard; calendaring them keeps a financing obligation from becoming a default nobody saw coming.
