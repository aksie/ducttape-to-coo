## Claim
A new hire's first days set the tone for how they perceive the culture and their entire time at the company — the impression formed in the first week is sticky and hard to change.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Gilboa: "Everything is new. Everything makes an impact. They either feel supported and excited or lost and bewildered. They feel initiated or left out. In a way, this sets the tone for how they perceive your culture and the rest of their time at the company."

## Why (if explicit in source)
First impressions in a new job are formed under heightened emotional attention — people are more alert, more sensitive to signals. The patterns they notice in week one become their mental model of the company that they'll use to interpret everything that comes after.
