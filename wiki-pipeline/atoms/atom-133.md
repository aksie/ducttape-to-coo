## Claim

A disorganised data room creates a bad first impression that makes investors and buyers more critical of everything that follows — even if the underlying business is sound.

## Source quote or paraphrase

A poorly organised data room creates a bad first impression, and a bad first impression makes buyers more critical of everything that follows. I applied this instinct from years on the buy side.

## Why

Investors and buyers use data room quality as a proxy for operational quality. Disorganisation signals that other things may also be poorly managed. The effect is disproportionate: a first impression formed in the first hour of a data room review influences interpretation of everything seen afterwards.
