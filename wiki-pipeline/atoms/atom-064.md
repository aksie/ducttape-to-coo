## Claim
You have an accountant engaged who can answer recurring strategic-finance questions ("Can our cash flow support a new hire?", "How much should we set aside for April taxes?").

## Source quote or paraphrase
patio11 recommends getting an accountant early, ideally one who works with similar service firms, and lists ongoing questions the accountant should be able to answer.

## Why
At First Hires stage, decisions about adding people or committing to recurring costs have outsized cash-flow consequences. Most founders lack the training to model these trade-offs themselves, and getting them wrong can mean missed payroll. An accountant familiar with similar businesses pattern-matches faster than a generalist.
