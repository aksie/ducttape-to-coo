## Claim

When friends, family, or first angels invest, use a sensible template for founder/shareholder terms that documents your principles and non-negotiables — not a blank slate for investors to invent.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Use a sensible template; principles and non-negotiables on paper beat 'we have nothing, you tell us what to put in the contract.'

## Why

Documented principles strengthen your position in negotiations and signal professionalism; a professional follow-on round will often replace documents anyway, so bespoke premium work at FFF is usually wasted.
