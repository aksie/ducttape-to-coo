## Claim
For investor reporting, tools like Visible.vc, Briefcase, or a consistent Google Slides template reduce the prep time for monthly updates significantly compared to ad hoc formats.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Visible.vc naturally recommends their own tooling, but the underlying point — that consistent templates reduce reporting overhead — is sound and source-neutral.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Format consistency means you're updating numbers, not rebuilding structure. A consistent template also makes period-over-period comparison easier for investors.
