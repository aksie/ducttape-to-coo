## Claim

Combine systems in order ("back door closed" — more a CISO than a legal job) with awareness training at the front, since most big leaks are human error; cyber insurance almost always requires awareness training anyway.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Two sides: back door closed (CISO work) plus awareness training (human error causes most leaks); cyber insurance almost always requires awareness training. Back door closed + awareness = lowest chance of trouble.

## Why

Technical controls and human awareness cover the two ways data leaks; doing both is also what insurers demand before they'll cover you.
