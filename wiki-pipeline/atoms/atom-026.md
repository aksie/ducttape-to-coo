## Claim
Run a weekly check-in on company goals — not just a quarterly review. Ask: what progress was made, what's blocking us, where do we need help?

## Source quote or paraphrase
Davis structures weekly management meetings around OKRs: quick wins from the past week, progress against goals, what's keeping people up at night, where they need help. This cadence is what makes goals actionable rather than decorative.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Quarterly-only goal reviews turn goals into a once-a-quarter ritual with no operational connection to daily work. Weekly cadence keeps goals as the actual decision-making filter.
