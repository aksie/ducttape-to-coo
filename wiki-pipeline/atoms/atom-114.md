## Claim

Document your working hours policy explicitly from the first hire — even if your setup is flexible or remote.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Defining core hours, flex windows, and remote rules in writing protects the company and sets clear expectations before misunderstandings arise.

## Why (inferred from source if not explicit)

Without a written policy, working hours default to whatever each person assumes, which diverges fast in hybrid or remote setups. When conflicts arise — overtime disputes, burnout claims, tax authority questions — the absence of a policy shifts liability to the employer.
