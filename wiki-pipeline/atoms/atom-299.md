## Claim

Your first in-house lawyer spends the first months diagnosing what is actually needed — operationally and especially strategically — before being plugged into the operational firehose; a deliberate short mandate ("I'm only here for two months") forces that diagnostic posture.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Use the first months to diagnose what's needed (operationally and strategically) before the firehose; a two-month mandate forces a diagnostic posture, because hiring someone full-time opens the floodgates.

## Why

The moment someone is full-time the day-to-day work floods in, so a deliberate diagnostic window is the only way to surface the big strategic risks first.
