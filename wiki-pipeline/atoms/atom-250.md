## Claim

You should run a monthly written update before you have external investors — even if the only recipients are you and the leadership team.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner: deferring reporting until shareholders exist lets founders stay aligned through informal channels without confronting slips. Audience is secondary; the written discipline is primary.

## Why

External investors are an audience layer on a habit you need anyway. Starting early means you are not inventing a reporting process under fundraising or board pressure later.
