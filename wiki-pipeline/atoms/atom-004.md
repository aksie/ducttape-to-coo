## Claim
Do a monthly variance review — actual vs. forecast — and write down why significant variances happened. The variance explanation matters more than the forecast itself.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Lenny emphasises that variance analysis builds forecast accuracy over time. Understanding why you were wrong is the feedback loop that makes the next forecast better.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Forecasts that are never compared to actuals never improve. The discipline of explaining variances forces you to understand your business model more deeply.
