## Claim
Reconcile credit card statements against receipts before every month-end close — if you skip this step, your P&L will contain uncategorised transactions and your books will never close cleanly.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Founderpath covers that credit card statements should be reconciled against receipts before month-end close, alongside completing bank reconciliation monthly rather than quarterly.

## Why
Credit card transactions are the most common source of missing expense data. If you close the month without matching every card transaction to a receipt and a category, those transactions sit as "uncategorised" and your financial statements are incomplete. Over multiple months, this compounds into a growing mess of orphaned transactions that makes your books unreliable for decision-making.
