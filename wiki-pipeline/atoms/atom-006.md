## Claim
Document your revenue recognition policy before signing any enterprise contracts — retroactive policy changes are painful and create restatement risk.

## Source quote or paraphrase
a16z flags revenue recognition as one of the most commonly deferred finance decisions, and one of the most expensive to fix retroactively when you scale or enter diligence.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Enterprise contracts often have milestone payments, multi-year terms, or service components that require judgment calls on when revenue is recognised. Without a documented policy, different team members handle these inconsistently.
