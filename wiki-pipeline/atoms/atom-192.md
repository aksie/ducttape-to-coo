## Claim

At your stage, you should default to asynchronous communication and treat real-time chat, calls, and in-person conversation as exceptions when async is too slow or too risky.

## Source quote or paraphrase

37signals: "Real-time sometimes, asynchronous most of the time."

## Why

Async reduces interruption during maker work; reserving sync for true back-and-forth prevents chat from becoming an always-on obligation that scales poorly past a handful of people.
