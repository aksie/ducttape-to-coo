## Claim

On day one of notice, you should acknowledge the departure to the team lead, set the last day, and assign a transition owner who will receive knowledge handoffs.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Tinyteam day-1 steps: notify stakeholders, confirm last day, assign transition owner before week-1 knowledge transfer sessions begin.

## Why

Naming a transition owner prevents handover from defaulting to the busiest founder. Even at five people, someone must be explicitly responsible for receiving tribal knowledge.
