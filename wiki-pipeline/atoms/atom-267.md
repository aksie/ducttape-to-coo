## Claim

Annual accounts are properly adopted by a shareholders' resolution signed by all shareholders, the accounts are signed by the board, and they are filed on time — at minimum the CFO knows the filing status.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Accounts adopted per a shareholders' resolution and filed on time; the resolution signed by all shareholders, the accounts signed by the Board of Directors.

## Why

Adoption is a formal act; doing it correctly and on time is what keeps directors protected and the company's filings defensible later.
