## Claim
Complete a bank reconciliation every month — not quarterly. Unreconciled accounts hide errors that compound over time.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Founderpath describes monthly bank reconciliation as a basic financial control that many early-stage companies skip or do quarterly, creating a growing pool of undetected errors.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Errors found monthly are small and quick to fix. Errors found quarterly are larger, harder to trace, and sometimes unresolvable without reconstructing months of transactions.
