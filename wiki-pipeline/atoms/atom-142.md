## Claim

File WBSO (R&D tax credit) applications and decisions in a dedicated folder, and treat them as a material document for any investor or buyer due diligence.

## Source quote or paraphrase

WBSO applications and decisions filed in /compliance/grants-and-subsidies/wbso/. These are valuable in a DD process as they demonstrate R&D activity.

## Why

WBSO documentation serves two roles in due diligence: it supports the tax position (showing R&D credits were properly claimed) and it demonstrates substantive technical activity to a buyer evaluating IP and product claims. Missing documentation means both the credit history and the R&D narrative need to be reconstructed from scratch.
