## Claim

You have hired someone with no employment or contractor contract because you trust the relationship.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Do not hire people with no contract because you trust the vibe.

## Why

When relationships sour or diligence arrives, missing contracts create liability exposure and IP uncertainty that are expensive to fix retroactively.
