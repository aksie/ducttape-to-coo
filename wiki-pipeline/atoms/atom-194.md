## Claim

You should treat chat as the channel for movement and coordination, not as the company's memory.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Buteau: "Use chat for movement, not memory. If a decision, policy, or important context emerges there, move it to a durable record."

## Why

Chat is optimized for speed and social noise; decisions buried in threads become unfindable within weeks, forcing repeated questions and conflicting versions of truth.
