## Claim

You paid premium legal fees for a fully bespoke founder package at the first friends-and-family round when a professional round is likely soon.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Do not pay premium legal fees for a full bespoke package at the first FFF round if a professional round is likely soon — the next investor will often want their own documents.

## Why

Early bespoke documents are frequently discarded at the next financing; spend enough to get IP in and principles documented, not for transaction-grade polish too soon.
