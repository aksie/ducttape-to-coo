## Claim

When cross-team fights about mentions or channels surface, run a short joint session with CS and engineering leads — name each complaint, agree a shared path (channel or ticket type, @-mention rules, response window), write it in one paragraph, and post where both teams look.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner: 30–45 minute session; specifics are context-dependent; failure mode is leaving conflict as morale instead of an explicit norm decision.

## Why

The right SLA and channel map vary by product and team size. The non-negotiable is that **both** sides accept the same written handoff — otherwise the fight returns every quarter.
