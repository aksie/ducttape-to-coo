## Claim

A clean entity exists and all IP — code, brand, product, content — sits in the company or intended structure, not on an individual founder's laptop.

## Source quote or paraphrase

All IP is in the company. Not mostly, not except the first commits. Whatever happens, IP sits in the venture.

## Why

IP outside the entity is among the most common due-diligence and cap-table problems; investors expect a clean chain of title from incorporation onward.
