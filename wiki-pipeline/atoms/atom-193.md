## Claim

You should make substantial decisions in writing (complete thoughts in a doc or comment thread), not as one-line chat exchanges.

## Source quote or paraphrase

"Writing solidifies, chat dissolves. Substantial decisions start and end with an exchange of complete thoughts, not one-line-at-a-time jousts."

## Why

Chat fragments reasoning and disappears from search; written decisions stay legible to people who were not in the room and to future hires.
