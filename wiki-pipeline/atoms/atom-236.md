## Claim

You should hold two separate conversations near departure: a neutral exit interview for honest feedback, and a manager-led closure conversation focused on appreciation — not combined into one meeting.

## Source quote or paraphrase

OpenOrg explicitly separates data-gathering exit interview (People Ops or neutral party) from manager closure conversation celebrating contributions.

## Why

Combining them produces polite non-answers in the interview and awkward criticism in the goodbye. Splitting lets you learn and still end the relationship well.
