## Claim
Establish a fixed board reporting cadence and template before you close your Series A — don't wait until investors ask for it.

## Source quote or paraphrase
a16z notes that companies that establish reporting rhythm before closing a round build investor trust faster and spend less time on ad hoc requests post-close.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Ad hoc investor requests signal operational immaturity and consume disproportionate founder time. A consistent cadence resets expectations and reduces total reporting overhead.
