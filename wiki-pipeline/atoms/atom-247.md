## Claim

You should use a simple knowledge-transfer template — a Google Doc or Notion page listing daily tasks, key contacts, project status, and open loops — that the departing employee fills during notice period.

## Source quote or paraphrase

OpenOrg recommends standardised Notion or Google Doc template started 2–3 weeks before last day; includes state-of-role memo with priorities and recommended next steps for successor.

## Why

A blank doc is intimidating; a template with headings prompts the tacit knowledge that would otherwise stay in someone's head. At first hires you do not need a knowledge base — you need one good handover doc per departure.
