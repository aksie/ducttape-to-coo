## Claim

If you cannot point to last month's stated goals and how you scored against them, you do not have a reporting process — you have a newsletter.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Template requires lookback checkboxes tied to quarterly goals or prior update; without that link updates lack accountability loop.

## Why

Highlights-only emails feel good but do not force learning. The process entry failure is skipping the lookback section entirely.
