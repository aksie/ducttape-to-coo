## Claim
At First Hires, everyone on the team can tell you the company's top 3 priorities right now without looking anything up — goals are verbal, visible, and reviewed weekly.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Angus Davis describes the pre-OKR state at Swipely as still functional at small size because the CEO could hold goals in their head and repeat them constantly. The transition to a written system only became necessary around 30 people.

## Why (if explicit in source)
When the team is 2-10 people, the goal isn't a system — it's shared understanding. The goal of any planning process at this stage is alignment, not administration.
