## Claim
Run a rolling 13-week cash flow forecast updated weekly — this is your operational heartbeat at early scale.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Lenny describes the 13-week (quarterly) rolling cash forecast as the single most important operational finance habit at this stage. It gives enough forward visibility to act on problems before they become crises.

## Why (if explicit in source)
13 weeks gives enough runway visibility to make decisions — hiring, spending, fundraising timing — while being short enough to forecast with reasonable accuracy.
