## Claim

If you have never live-tested your offboarding checklist — timing how long revocation takes and which services you forget — you will discover gaps during a real departure under stress.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN practitioner who had just onboarded used fresh knowledge of every documented service to test revocation on their own account before executing it for a departing employee.

## Why

The founder is usually the only person who knows all the tools. Without a documented, tested checklist, each departure becomes a panicked scavenger hunt that pulls you out of the business for a day.
