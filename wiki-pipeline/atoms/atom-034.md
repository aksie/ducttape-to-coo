## Claim
If you don't know your company's growth rate right now, you don't know if you're doing well or badly — you're flying without instruments.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Paul Graham: "If there's one number every founder should always know, it's the company's growth rate. That's the measure of a startup. If you don't know that number, you don't even know if you're doing well or badly."

## Why (if explicit in source)
Growth rate is the leading indicator of everything else. Without it, you're evaluating company health based on feel, which is unreliable and easy to deceive yourself with.
