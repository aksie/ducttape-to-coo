## Claim

You should use meetings sparingly — as a last resort when writing and async exchange are insufficient for sensitivity, speed, or back-and-forth.

## Source quote or paraphrase

37signals: "Meetings are the last resort, not the first option."

## Why

A five-person hour meeting costs five person-hours; defaulting to meetings for what a doc could resolve burns the team's scarcest resource at small headcount.
