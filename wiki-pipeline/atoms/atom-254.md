## Claim

When angels or friends-and-family investors arrive, you should widen distribution on the same monthly template — not design a new reporting process from scratch.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner: when you raise, you are widening distribution on a habit that already keeps you honest.

## Why

Inventing reporting during a round adds friction and produces shallow first editions. Same structure, new BCC list.
