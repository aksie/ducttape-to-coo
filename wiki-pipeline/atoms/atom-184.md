## Claim

Tighten your legal relationship at concrete triggers: first hire imminent, first external term sheet, first large customer contract needing real terms, or move into regulated or data-sensitive territory.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Good moments to tighten the relationship: first hire, first term sheet, first large customer contract, regulated or data-sensitive product.

## Why

These moments shift risk profile quickly; having counsel who will also tell you when not to legalise yet prevents both under- and over-shooting.
