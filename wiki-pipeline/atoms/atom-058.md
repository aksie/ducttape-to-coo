## Claim
Document your expense policy in writing and have every employee acknowledge it — verbal policies do not survive as the team grows, and you will lose control of spend within weeks.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Founderpath emphasises that expense policies must be documented and signed by all employees, arguing that verbal policies don't survive diligence or team growth.

## Why
When a policy only exists in the founder's head or in verbal conversations, every new hire learns different rules. Within weeks of hiring, you will have employees spending at different thresholds, submitting different types of expenses, and expecting different reimbursement processes. A written policy that everyone acknowledges creates a single source of truth and gives you grounds to enforce boundaries consistently.
