## Claim

You have commissioned an extensive shareholder agreement or spent thousands on legal before anyone has paid for the product.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Do not spend thousands on an 80-page shareholder agreement before you know if anyone will pay a euro.

## Why

Large upfront legal cost and complexity before validation is a common way founders burn runway and founder time on paperwork instead of learning whether the idea works.
