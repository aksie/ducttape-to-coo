## Claim

You should have a written offboarding checklist with a named owner for each workstream — IT access, knowledge transfer, equipment, HR paperwork, and team communication — before anyone gives notice.

## Source quote or paraphrase

OpenOrg guide assigns DRIs per item: manager owns handover, IT owns access and assets, People Ops coordinates. Ambiguity is called the enemy of smooth offboarding.

## Why

At 2–10 people there is no HR department to absorb improvisation. A one-page checklist prevents the founder from reconstructing the process from memory during an emotionally charged departure.
