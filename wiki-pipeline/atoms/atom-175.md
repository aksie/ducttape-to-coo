## Claim

If you build a digital product that collects personal data, design privacy and GDPR compliance from minute one — what you collect, why, how it is stored, and processor agreements where needed.

## Source quote or paraphrase

For digital businesses collecting personal data: treat privacy/GDPR seriously from minute one.

## Why

Regulators and professional investors review data practices; retrofitting after a year of collection is slower, riskier, and can block investment entirely.
