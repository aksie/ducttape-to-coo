## Claim

You should use email for external parties (customers, vendors, investors) and keep Slack for internal coordination unless you have a shared external channel by agreement.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Multiple HN commenters: email remains the interface with outsiders; internal quick coordination lives in chat.

## Why

External comms need permanence, forwarding, and boundaries; mixing them into internal chat blurs audit trails and pulls the whole team into client noise.
