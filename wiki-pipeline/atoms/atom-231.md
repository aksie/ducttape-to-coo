## Claim

When someone leaves, you should remove them from the password manager first, then rotate every shared credential they could access — in that order, the same day.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN operator workflow: kick user out of 1Password, change passwords they had access to, save new passwords in manager so remaining team picks them up automatically without realising they changed.

## Why

Removing access stops new reads; rotation stops any password they already copied. Most companies skip rotation because churn is low — that is exactly when one departure creates outsized risk.
