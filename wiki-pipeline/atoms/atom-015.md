## Claim
Gross margin should be calculated and reported from the first month of revenue — not introduced later when it becomes a board agenda item.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Visible.vc argues that gross margin is a core health metric, not a growth-stage metric. Companies that don't track it early are often surprised by how thin it is when they finally calculate it properly.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Gross margin trends reveal unit economics before they become a problem at scale. A margin that is declining slowly from month 3 is invisible if you only start tracking it at month 18.
