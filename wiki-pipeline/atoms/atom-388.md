## Claim

For senior hires, use three conversations: hiring manager (fit and chemistry), one level up (potential only, no veto), long-tenure culture carrier (no solo veto).

## Source quote or paraphrase

Three conversations: hiring manager; one up (potential, no veto); culture carrier (no solo veto).

## Why

Separating fit, potential, and culture without solo vetoes reduces binary not-a-fit failures.
