## Claim

You should structure every update with highlights, misses/help up front, checkbox lookback against prior goals, and checkbox lookahead for the next period.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Investor-update template: misses and asks before polish; lookback uses [x]/[ ] against quarterly goals or last update; lookahead by product, growth, finance.

## Why

Up-front misses build investor trust. Checkbox format makes next month's lookback automatic — you are always scoring what you said you would do.
