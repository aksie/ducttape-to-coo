## Claim

The management-to-statutory bridge is consistently the most time-consuming item to reconstruct in a due diligence process — and it almost never exists when needed.

## Source quote or paraphrase

The management reporting bridge is consistently the most time-consuming item to reconstruct in a due diligence process. It's also the easiest to maintain year-round if you simply write it down each time you prepare the annual accounts.

## Why

Reconstructing the bridge under deal pressure requires re-examining every revenue recognition decision, every EBITDA adjustment, and every non-recurring item across multiple years — often with team members who have left or with accounting records that weren't set up to produce this view. Writing it contemporaneously, when the reasoning is fresh, takes a fraction of the time.
