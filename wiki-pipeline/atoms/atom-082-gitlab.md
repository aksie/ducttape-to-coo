## Claim
At scaled stage, assign review rhythms to planning layers: strategy reviewed annually, annual goals reviewed quarterly, and quarterly OKRs reviewed monthly.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab says each planning element is reviewed at the cadence one level below it, including strategy annually, yearlies quarterly, and OKRs monthly.

## Why
Planning artifacts become stale unless they have a maintenance rhythm. Review cadence keeps priorities current without waiting for a full planning reset.
