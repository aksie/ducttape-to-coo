## Claim
Pick one growth metric and use it as the filter for every decision: if an action helps hit your growth target this week, do it; if not, deprioritise it.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Paul Graham: "Focusing on hitting a growth rate reduces the otherwise bewilderingly multifarious problem of starting a startup to a single problem. You can use that target growth rate to make all your decisions for you."

## Why (if explicit in source)
Startups face too many competing priorities to evaluate each one on its merits. A single growth metric acts as a tiebreaker and prevents the paralysis of endless strategic debate.
