## Claim
For a remote-first early team, your reimbursable expense list should focus on enabling work: laptop, peripherals, headset, desk, chair, internet, phone, calling credit, and office space if home is impractical.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab lists work equipment and services it will pay for when needed for work and allowed by local law.

## Why
At this phase, the useful policy question is not procurement optimization; it is whether people have the basic setup needed to work effectively.
