## Claim

You should store team credentials in a cloud password manager with per-person access — not shared spreadsheets, Slack messages, or local KeePass files on Google Drive.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN password thread: use 1Password collections by role; never shared spreadsheets; remove departing user from manager first then rotate shared passwords; KeePass-on-Drive is too easy to copy and hard to audit.

## Why

Offboarding at small companies fails most often on shared passwords the team forgot someone knew. A manager with access logs tells you exactly which creds to rotate when one person leaves.
