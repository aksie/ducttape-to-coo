## Claim
Reconcile all bank accounts and credit cards every month — with proper tooling, it takes less than 2 hours and gives you confidence that your numbers are real.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Bench covers that monthly reconciliation of all accounts takes less than 2 hours with proper tooling, and is foundational to accurate bookkeeping.

## Why
Monthly reconciliation is the single most important control to catch errors early — duplicate transactions, missed deposits, bank fees you didn't expect, or fraudulent charges. If you only reconcile quarterly, you have 3 months of transactions to untangle and any problem is 3x harder to trace back. At the first-hires stage, the volume is low enough that monthly reconciliation is genuinely quick; if you let it slide, it becomes a dreaded quarterly marathon.
