## Claim

Before sending an offer letter, get explicit CFO or COO approval that the specific salary fits the band agreed when the role was approved.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner: within hiring there must be OK from CFO or COO to send out an offer letter with an amount that fits the budget.

## Why

The offer is where compensation becomes binding in practice. Separating "approved role band" from "approved offer amount" closes the gap where managers negotiate above band without finance visibility.
