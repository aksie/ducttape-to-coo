## Claim

If you only think about offboarding tasks on the employee's last day — access, equipment, handover, and paperwork all at once — you are behind and will leave gaps.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Tinyteam and multiple sources describe compressing all phases into last day as the most common small-company offboarding failure; checklist is explicitly phased across notice period.

## Why

Last-day-only offboarding guarantees missed knowledge transfer and rushed revocation. The warning sign is recognizable: you have no checklist and no work started until the morning they leave.
