## Claim

You can say whether any litigation is running and triage it — rather than filtering on "material," since a single high-stakes settlement agreement (VSO) can itself be material.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Hard to tie litigation to an amount; a single VSO can be material — better to ask "is there any litigation running?" and triage.

## Why

A "material only" filter hides cases that turn out to matter; knowing everything that's running lets you judge significance deliberately.
