## Claim

Self-assessment of your own DD readiness is unreliable. Have an external legal advisor review your filing before opening the data room to investors or buyers — even if you have M&A experience yourself.

## Source quote or paraphrase

I thought I was well prepared. Then I went through everything with our external legal advisor — and discovered I wasn't. Missing addenda confirming salary changes. Contracts that hadn't actually been signed. Documents filed inconsistently. It was a solid week of work to get things properly in order.

## Why

Founders know the context behind every document and unconsciously fill in gaps that an external reviewer would flag. An experienced advisor sees the filing the way a buyer's legal team will: as signal. The week of remediation before opening the data room is far cheaper than the negotiating damage from a buyer discovering the same gaps.
