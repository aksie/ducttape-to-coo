## Claim

At pre-traction, you and your co-founder have talked through the basics on paper — roles, capital contribution, and what happens if it is not working — without needing heavyweight legal documents yet.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Talk through basics on a napkin: who does what, who puts in money, when to pull the plug. Nothing super legal yet, but zero paper among co-builders is foolish.

## Why

Handshake clarity prevents silent misalignment while you validate the idea; full legal packages before PMF waste money and founder time.
