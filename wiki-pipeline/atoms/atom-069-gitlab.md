## Claim
Even with a trust-based policy, set a simple expense reporting deadline and a hard rule for personal spend on company cards.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab requires expense reports seven days after quarter-end, and personal card charges must be reported quickly and refunded within 14 days.

## Why
The policy keeps the process light, but still protects basic financial hygiene: receipts need to arrive on a predictable cadence, and accidental personal use cannot linger unresolved.
