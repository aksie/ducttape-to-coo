## Claim

You are behind if CS and engineering are fighting about how to communicate in chat — engineers say @-mentions in random channels kill deep work; CS says ignored mentions mean customers suffer — and nobody has agreed a handoff path both teams can operate under.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner contribution: predictable fight when CS and engineering split without norms; both sides often right; missing piece is joint decision on channels/tickets/mentions/SLA, not a universal prescription.

## Why

Customer impact and engineering throughput both degrade when escalation is ad hoc. Morale blame replaces an ops decision the company should have made explicitly.
