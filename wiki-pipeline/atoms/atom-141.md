## Claim

File signed annual accounts as a PDF within two weeks of the AGM sign-off, and keep the last five years accessible in a known location.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Statutory annual accounts (jaarrekening) signed off by the AGM and filed with KvK within the legal deadline. Store as signed PDFs: /finance/annual-accounts/[year]-annual-accounts.pdf. Keep last 5 years accessible.

## Why

Annual accounts are one of the first documents requested in any due diligence process. A complete, organised set of the last three to five years — immediately accessible — signals financial professionalism. Missing years or unsigned copies trigger follow-up questions and erode confidence.
