## Claim

File every employment contract as a signed PDF in a central location from the moment of first hire — not just for key employees, but for all employees. Include non-compete, non-solicitation, confidentiality, and IP assignment as standard clauses, not optional add-ons.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Every employee: signed contract filed in /hr/employment-contracts/[name]/. Start with key employees — if you have 20 people and only 10 contracts are on file, start with highest-paid and longest-tenured. Non-compete, NDA, and IP assignment should be in the standard contract, not optional add-ons.

## Why

In HR due diligence, buyers review contracts for completeness, consistency, and IP coverage. Gaps — especially missing IP assignment clauses — are material findings. The work to retrofit these clauses into existing contracts is significant and involves re-signing; adding them to the standard template from the start costs nothing.
