## Claim

If you revoke access but never audit what credentials the departing employee may have saved outside company systems, you have only done half the security job.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN manager story: developer whose access was revoked later defaced the website using a WordPress password saved in personal Google Drive — appeared stable, actions were surprising.

## Why

Revocation stops company-controlled access paths, not personal copies. The warning sign is assuming good people means no residual risk — offboarding must include rotating any shared creds they touched.
