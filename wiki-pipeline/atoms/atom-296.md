## Claim

If you develop or deploy high-risk AI systems, get expert advice — the AI Act sets strict rules. For example, a recruitment tool that auto-screens CVs before a human is involved can be high-risk (discrimination risk), so building one yourself should trigger consulting an expert.

## Source quote or paraphrase

## Why

High-risk AI carries strict legal obligations and discrimination exposure that a general scan can't resolve — it needs a specialist before you ship.
