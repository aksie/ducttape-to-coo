## Claim

You should discuss work on the artifact it concerns — task comment, doc comment, or ticket — not in a detached channel where context gets lost.

## Source quote or paraphrase

37signals: "If we're discussing a specific task, we discuss it in the comment section below the task itself… communications stay attached to the thing we're discussing."

## Why

Detached discussions fragment the story; the next person sees a conclusion without the reasoning or the file it applied to.
