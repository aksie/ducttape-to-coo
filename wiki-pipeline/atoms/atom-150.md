## Claim

When transitioning from folder-based HR filing to an HR system (Personio, AFAS, or similar), designate one source of truth explicitly and migrate fully — maintaining both in parallel creates inconsistencies that are hard to untangle later.

## Source quote or paraphrase

The transition from folder-based to system-based filing needs to be clean — don't maintain both in parallel without a clear "source of truth" designation.

## Why

Parallel systems diverge silently. A contract updated in the HR system but not in the folder, or vice versa, means neither is reliable. In a due diligence process, the question "where is the signed contract for this employee?" should have exactly one answer. If the answer is "probably in one of two places," you have a problem.
