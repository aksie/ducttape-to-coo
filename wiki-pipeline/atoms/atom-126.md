## Claim

Collect emergency contact details from every employee during onboarding — store them in HR records, not in the handbook itself.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Emergency contacts (noodcontact) should be collected at onboarding and stored where HR or management can access them quickly. The handbook should reference where this information lives and how to update it — not publish the contacts themselves.

## Why (inferred from source if not explicit)

In a medical emergency or personal crisis, knowing who to contact and how fast you can reach them is critical. At 5-10 people this feels unnecessary — until it isn't. Storing them centrally (not in Slack DMs or a manager's head) means they're accessible to whoever is present when needed.
