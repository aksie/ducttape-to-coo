## Claim

You should plan at least one in-person team gathering per year for relationship-building; treat video or chat as the default for day-to-day work.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN remote practitioners: difference between zero and one annual meetup is substantial; quarterly in-person is often too heavy on personal lives.

## Why

Remote teams lose empathy and informal context over time; occasional in-person time improves async efficiency afterward without making travel the operating norm.
