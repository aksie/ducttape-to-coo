## Claim
At growth stage, planning should become a cadence system across different time horizons: week, month, quarter, year, strategy, vision, and mission.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab's 2019 cadence page lists company cadences from day and week through quarter, year, strategy, vision, and mission.

## Why
Growth adds recurring planning events that cannot be coordinated from a single quarterly goal list. Cadence turns planning into an operating rhythm.
