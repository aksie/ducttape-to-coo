## Claim

Set up an internal authority matrix — who may do and sign what, with thresholds by area of responsibility plus a monetary amount, approved by board or shareholder resolution — and register a power of attorney (volmacht) with the KvK where you need third-party effect (derdenwerking); without registration it is internal only.

## Source quote or paraphrase

An authority matrix: levels with area-of-responsibility + monetary thresholds, approved by resolution; registering a volmacht with KvK gives derdenwerking, otherwise it's internal only (though apparent authority may still bind you).

## Why

A signing policy spreads responsibility off the statutory directors and makes signers accountable; KvK registration is what makes the delegation effective against third parties.
