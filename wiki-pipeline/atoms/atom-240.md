## Claim

If you skip exit interviews because the departure is amicable or you do not want an awkward conversation, you lose the only unfiltered feedback loop you have at this stage.

## Source quote or paraphrase

OpenOrg and tinyteam both note exit interviews are underused at small companies when partings feel friendly; patterns across multiple exits are where actionable data lives.

## Why

First hires rarely have engagement surveys or HR analytics. Exit interviews are your cheapest attrition diagnostic — skipping them means repeated hiring mistakes.
