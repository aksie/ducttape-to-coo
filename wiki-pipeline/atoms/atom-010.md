## Claim
If your runway is under 18 months, review cash position weekly — not monthly. Monthly cadence gives you too little lead time to act.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Several bootstrapped HN founders emphasise weekly cash review when runway is tight. Monthly cadence means you can be 4 weeks into a problem before you see it.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Runway problems compound. Discovering a problem 4 weeks earlier gives you meaningfully more options: adjusting spend, accelerating sales, or starting a fundraise from a position of relative strength.
