## Claim
Introduce new hires to every function in the company before they get buried in their day-to-day work — even 30-minute conversations with people in other teams can prevent siloing later.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Loftesness: "This is the time to introduce them to each function — the data team, the tools team, the sales team, the product team — before they get buried in their day-to-day work. Fostering an understanding of what each team does, the basic act of putting names to faces after a meaningful interaction, is a great way to sidestep factions down the road."

## Why (if explicit in source)
Factions form when people only know their own team's perspective. Cross-team introductions during onboarding create empathy before the pressures of daily work make collaboration feel like a cost rather than a natural thing.
