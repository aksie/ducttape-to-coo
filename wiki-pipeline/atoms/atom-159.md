## Claim

Incorporate when you have real momentum — users, revenue, or serious outside interest to invest or partner — not on day one of the idea.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Move when there is real momentum: users, revenue, or serious interest from outsiders who want to invest or partner.

## Why

Incorporating too early adds cost and distraction; incorporating too late forces messy transfer of valuable IP or goodwill into the entity.
