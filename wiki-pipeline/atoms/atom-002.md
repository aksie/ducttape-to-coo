## Claim
Your financial model should function as an operational decision-making tool, not a document you update before fundraising rounds.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Lenny argues that most founders build models reactively for investors rather than proactively for themselves — the model exists in their head or in a pitch deck, not as a live operational tool.

## Why (if explicit in source)
If the model only gets updated for investors, the numbers in it reflect what you want to show, not what's actually happening. Operational models built for internal use develop better forecast accuracy over time.
