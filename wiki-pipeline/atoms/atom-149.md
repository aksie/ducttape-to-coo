## Claim

Maintain standard, lawyer-reviewed templates for offer letters and freelance agreements. Use them consistently — don't draft new contracts from scratch for each hire.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Standard templates for offer letters and freelance agreements reviewed by a lawyer at least once, updated when the law changes, and used consistently.

## Why

Ad hoc contracts introduce inconsistencies — different IP clauses, different non-compete formulations, different notice periods — that create complexity in due diligence and potential legal exposure. A standard template reviewed once is far cheaper than fixing inconsistencies across 20 contracts after the fact.
