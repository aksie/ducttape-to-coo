## Claim

Whenever something important is decided in Slack, you should codify it in your wiki or handbook — chat is not the system of record.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN: if anything is decided in Slack, it has to be codified somewhere else, like a wiki; Slack should not be the source of truth.

## Why

Slack search decays as volume grows; without codification, the same debates repeat and new hires cannot self-serve answers.
