## Claim

For remote departures, you should generate and send a prepaid shipping label for company equipment — laptop, phone, security keys — and track return against your asset list.

## Source quote or paraphrase

OpenOrg recommends signed asset-return checklist; HN ops practitioners describe company-generated FedEx labels as standard for remote offboarding with tracked inventory.

## Why

Equipment drift is slow-burn risk: lost laptops mean lost data and unnecessary hardware cost. A label removes friction so goodwill does not determine whether you get the device back.
