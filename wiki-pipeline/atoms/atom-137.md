## Claim

Maintain a running list of your top contracts (start with the top 10 by value — customers and suppliers), each with a one-line description of what it covers and a link to the signed PDF.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Maintain a /legal/contracts/customers/ and /legal/contracts/suppliers/ directory — not every contract, start with top 10 by value. Each entry: one-liner description, link to PDF.

## Why

In a due diligence process, buyers will ask for material contracts. Having a pre-structured list means you can respond immediately rather than reconstructing it from memory or email search. The one-liner per contract makes it possible for someone unfamiliar with the business to quickly understand what each agreement covers.
