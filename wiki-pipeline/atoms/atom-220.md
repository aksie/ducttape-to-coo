## Claim

Before an Ops or HR lead signs contracts on the company's behalf, agree in writing which contract types they may sign and set up formal signing authority from the director.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Have a lightweight process in place where you agree in what cases the Ops lead can sign labour contracts, and set the legal framework (power of attorney style from formal director/CEO) so the contracts are legally valid.

## Why

The legal instrument (typically a power of attorney in NL) makes delegated signatures binding. The written matrix of authorised contract types prevents scope creep — standard employment from approved templates yes, non-standard commercial terms no, until explicitly added.
