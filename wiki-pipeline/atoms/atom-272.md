## Claim

The roles of your company's bodies — board, supervisory board (RvC), advisory board (RvA), works council where present — are documented, and governance is arranged in practice, not just professionalised on paper.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Roles of the company's organs are documented; the path is RvA → at Series A/B usually an RvC; the test is whether it is actually arranged in practice, not only on paper.

## Why

Investors and a next round expect real governance; paper structures that nobody follows fail the "in practice" test exactly when it matters.
