## Claim
Prefix key results with an owner, and accept that lower-level key results do not have to match higher-level goals perfectly.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab says OKRs are prefaced with the owner of the key result and that the match with the higher key result does not have to be perfect.

## Why
Ownership makes goals operational. Imperfect matching keeps the cascade practical instead of turning planning into taxonomy work.
