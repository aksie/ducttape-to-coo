## Claim
The next maturity step after cascading OKRs is to place OKRs inside a broader company cadence with board meetings, sales targets, metrics reviews, yearly planning, strategy, vision, and mission.

## Source quote or paraphrase
The 2019 cadence page lists OKRs as one quarterly item alongside board meetings, sales targets, and E-group offsites, within a larger set of time horizons.

## Why
OKRs coordinate goals, but they do not coordinate every recurring management process. A growing company needs the goal system and the operating calendar to fit together.
