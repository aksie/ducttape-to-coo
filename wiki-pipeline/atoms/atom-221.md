## Claim

Team leads are signing employment or vendor contracts without documented signing authority.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Don't let them sign contracts without them having a legal right to do so — as contract work moves from CEO/founder to team leads, authority is often assumed from the role rather than established formally.

## Why

Contracts signed by someone without power to bind the company may be invalid or challenged. Due diligence routinely surfaces improperly authorised signatures alongside missing contracts — both are expensive to fix retroactively.
