## Claim

The moment a departure date is confirmed — including early termination — you should notify whoever owns IT access so account expiry is updated immediately; never rely on the original contract end date alone.

## Source quote or paraphrase

NCS case post-mortem on HN: HR forgot to tell IT when a contractor left early; IT left account expiry on original contract date; contractor retained admin access weeks later from abroad.

## Why

At first hires HR and IT are often the same founder. Even then, write it down: departure confirmation triggers an IT ticket or checklist item, not an assumption someone will remember.
