## Claim

You should use Google Workspace (or equivalent) as your identity provider plus a team password manager such as 1Password or Bitwarden — this pairing covers most first-hires offboarding with two disable actions.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN security and password threads converge on Google SSO for SaaS plus cloud password manager with collections; Bitwarden/1Password both cited by practitioners at small-company scale.

## Why

These are the minimum viable stack for knowing who has access and removing it without enterprise IAM budget. Most startups already have Google Workspace from day one.
