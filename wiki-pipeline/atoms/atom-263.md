## Claim

Job openings are live on LinkedIn or job boards but nobody can point to who approved the budget or salary band for that role.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner failure: people posted not approved job openings.

## Why

If posting does not require a documented approval step, managers will post when understaffed — finance and leadership learn about new headcount from applicants, not from a plan.
