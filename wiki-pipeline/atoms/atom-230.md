## Claim

On involuntary termination, you should revoke all system access within the hour of the conversation — do not offer a grace window to download email or files first.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN practitioners with admin/root access describe standard practice: cut off completely right now; longer checklist for people with elevated access; no deferral for personal downloads.

## Why

Grace periods create exfiltration windows — HN managers report near-misses where lingering access almost enabled customer data theft. Speed is diligence, not disrespect, when the departure is contentious.
