## Claim

At growth stage, an archived set of monthly management reports (going back at least 18 months) becomes a standard investor and buyer expectation — not just the current year.

## Source quote or paraphrase

At growth, management reporting is more sophisticated, statutory accounts are more complex, and the gap between the two is larger and harder to explain without preparation. An audit trail of management reporting (all monthly reports, archived) becomes expected by investors and buyers.

## Why

Trend analysis and seasonality assessment require a time series of management reports. A buyer evaluating growth trajectory, churn patterns, or margin evolution needs historical data — not just what the current year looks like. Missing historical reports means the analysis has to be rebuilt from raw data, which adds weeks and creates inconsistencies.
