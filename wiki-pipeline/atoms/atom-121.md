## Claim

If you are too small for an internal vertrouwenspersoon, you can contract an external one — providers like Perspectief and GIMD offer this as a service.

## Source quote or paraphrase

External vertrouwenspersoon services are available for small Dutch companies that cannot designate an internal advisor. Providers include Perspectief (perspectief-vp.nl) and GIMD (gimd.nl).

## Why (inferred from source if not explicit)

An internal vertrouwenspersoon requires someone with sufficient seniority, time, and neutrality to handle sensitive reports — which is unrealistic at 10-15 people. An external provider solves the compliance requirement without adding a headcount obligation.
