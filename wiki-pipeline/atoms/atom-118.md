## Claim

Document your sick leave procedure in writing before your first employee starts — it is a legal requirement in the Netherlands, not a nice-to-have.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Dutch WVP requires a written sick leave notification process: who the employee reports to, by what time on day one, and what the company's role is at key milestones (day 6, week 6, week 42, year 1). This must be communicated to employees.

## Why (inferred from source if not explicit)

The WVP creates a structured timeline of obligations for both employer and employee during illness. Missing a milestone — even accidentally — can extend the re-integration obligation beyond two years and shift liability to the employer. A written procedure reduces the chance of a missed step when a situation is already stressful.
