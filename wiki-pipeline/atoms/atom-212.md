## Claim

You should keep sensitive topics in private channels or DMs — HR feedback, comp, credentials, fundraise/M&A — and treat everything else as public-by-default with minimal @-tags.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Vohra: sensitive topics are the minority (personnel, PII, delicate projects); valuable non-sensitive DMs should move to public channels.

## Why

Public-by-default improves discoverability and reduces duplicate questions; narrowing private comms prevents both gossip risk and unnecessary silos.
