## Claim
Write down how people should choose work: pick from the current milestone, keep issues actionable, and avoid taking work until you are actually starting it.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab tells people to pick issues from the current milestone and assign issues to themselves only when they start working.

## Why
Early planning fails when the work list becomes aspirational clutter. A simple milestone and assignment norm keeps planning close to actual execution.
