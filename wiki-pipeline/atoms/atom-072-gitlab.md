## Claim
At the foundation stage, company-level planning can be a shared strategy reference plus clear daily prioritization norms; it does not need to start as a formal OKR process.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab points team members to a strategy document and tells them to prioritize consciously based on urgency, impact, who is waiting, and how many people are affected.

## Why
When the team is small, alignment can travel through a shared document and explicit work-selection heuristics. The overhead of a full planning system would likely exceed the coordination benefit.
