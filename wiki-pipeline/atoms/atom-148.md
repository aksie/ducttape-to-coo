## Claim

From your first employee, designate a confidential counsellor (vertrouwenspersoon) — either internal or external — and communicate who this is to staff. This is a legal obligation under the Dutch Working Conditions Act (Arbowet), not optional.

## Source quote or paraphrase

From first hire: confidential counsellor (vertrouwenspersoon) — either internal or external — is a legal obligation under the Working Conditions Act (Arbowet) for companies with employees. Document who this is and that it's been communicated.

## Why

The Arbowet requires employers to have arrangements in place to address workplace misconduct, harassment, and unsafe situations. The vertrouwenspersoon is the designated point of contact for employees. Not having one is a compliance gap that surfaces in any HR audit or due diligence. It also leaves the company exposed if an employee raises an issue and there is no documented process for handling it.
