## Claim

You have been collecting user data for months without a coherent privacy policy, lawful basis, or technical controls.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Do not collect user data for a year and only then ask what your privacy policy should say.

## Why

Late privacy fixes often require product and data-architecture changes under time pressure; regulators and investors treat this as a stop sign.
