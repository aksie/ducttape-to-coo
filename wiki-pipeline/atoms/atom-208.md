## Claim

You should route interrupt-driven questions through a dedicated support-style channel with a weekly rotation and simple SLA — not your main team channel.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN practitioners use #support- channels with fixed rotation and SLA; redirect ad-hoc pings from the team channel.

## Why

Unbounded pings in team channels force everyone to stay interruptible; rotation makes the cost visible and protects deep work for the rest of the team.
