## Claim

Set rules for AI use: at a bare minimum, don't upload customer or employee databases into AI tools (treat sharing with an external AI as a potential data leak, even on a paid enterprise account), and ideally have an AI governance policy stating how and when AI may be used.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Bare minimum: don't upload customer/employee databases into AI tools; treat external AI sharing as a potential leak; ideally an AI governance policy.

## Why

Pasting personal data into an AI tool can be a data leak with the same consequences as any other breach; a policy makes the boundary explicit before someone crosses it.
