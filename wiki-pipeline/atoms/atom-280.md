## Claim

Have one employment contract checked by a lawyer early, before you scale hiring — your key early hires all sit on that first (often flawed) contract and are hard to move to stricter terms later because of the personal relationship.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Get one employment contract checked early; key early hires sit on the first, often flawed, contract and are hard to re-tighten later due to the relationship.

## Why

Fixing the template early is cheap; renegotiating tighter terms with people you now depend on is expensive and awkward — so the flaws tend to stay.
