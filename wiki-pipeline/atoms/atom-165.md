## Claim

After incorporation, meaningful IP, code, or brand assets still sit outside the company with individual founders.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Do not assume IP never killed a deal — keeping IP out of the entity causes unclean cap tables and DD pain.

## Why

Buyers and investors trace IP ownership; gaps force re-assignment, re-signing, or price adjustments at the worst moment.
