## Claim
At Growth stage, department heads should own their budget lines — financial accountability needs to be distributed, not centralised in the CEO or finance function.

## Source quote or paraphrase
a16z describes distributed budget ownership as the key structural shift in finance at the Growth stage. The finance function shifts from doing the work to enabling and holding accountable.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Centralised budget management doesn't scale. By 40-50 people you have too many cost centres for one person to manage effectively. Distributed ownership with central visibility is the mature model.
