## Claim
Give people a lightweight escalation norm: if they are unsure whether an expense is appropriate, they should inform their manager before the purchase or soon after it.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab says that when in doubt, team members should inform their manager before or after the purchase.

## Why
This preserves speed for obvious purchases while creating a low-friction path for judgment calls. It is not a formal approval workflow yet; it is a backstop for ambiguity.
