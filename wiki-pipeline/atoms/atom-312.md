## Claim

When a chief of staff or operator leaves, plan a successor who owns the rhythm — or execution reverts to founder intuition within a quarter.

## Source quote or paraphrase

When a CoS or operator leaves, a successor owns maintenance (medior CoS or equivalent).

## Why

Rhythm built by an operator decays quickly without a handoff owner; founders default back to corridor decisions.
