## Claim

Missing IP assignment agreements from early contractors and freelancers is one of the most common HR-related due diligence findings — and one of the hardest to fix retrospectively.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Missing IP assignment from early contractors is among the most common HR-related DD gaps that surfaces late.

## Why

Early-stage companies often engage contractors quickly and informally. The IP assignment question feels like a formality at the time. By the time a transaction is imminent, some of those contractors are unreachable, and getting a retrospective assignment signed is time-consuming, uncertain, and sometimes impossible. The missing assignment becomes a due diligence finding that creates uncertainty about ownership of core IP.
