## Claim
Create a lightweight commitment rule: do not make external promises without internal agreement, and keep the promises you do make.

## Source quote or paraphrase
GitLab says it keeps promises and does not make external promises without internal agreement.

## Why
At a small-team stage, over-promising is often a planning failure disguised as customer responsiveness. A simple promise rule protects focus and trust.
