## Claim

You should default to public team channels for operational questions and post any useful DM consensus back to a channel so the team can search it later.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN practitioner: "Everything public in Slack… if a DM happens and back and forth resulted in a consensus, share that consensus in a public channel."

## Why

DMs hide answers from the rest of the team and from future you; public-by-default builds a searchable shared brain without requiring everyone to read everything.
