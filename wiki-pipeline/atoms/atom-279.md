## Claim

Watch that employee IP assignment is actually safeguarded — it is the clause most often left unsafe — and that clauses you rely on are enforceable for the contract type you used (e.g. a non-compete is not enforceable everywhere).

## Source quote or paraphrase

Most often, IP assignment by employees isn't properly safeguarded; and some clauses (e.g. non-compete) aren't enforceable in all contract types.

## Why

If employee IP isn't assigned to the company, ownership of your product is unclear in DD; unenforceable clauses give false comfort until you try to rely on them.
