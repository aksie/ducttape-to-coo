## Claim
Separate COGS from operating expenses from the first month of revenue — retroactive separation is a significant analytical burden and delays your ability to see gross margin trends.

## Source quote or paraphrase
HN commenters repeatedly flag that separating COGS from opex early makes gross margin visible without a big analytical exercise. Retroactive recategorisation is described as "brutal."

## Why (if explicit in source)
Gross margin is a fundamental unit economics metric. You cannot optimise what you cannot see. Doing this from the start costs almost nothing; doing it retroactively costs significant time.
