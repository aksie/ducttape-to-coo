## Claim

A process exists to get as much relevant information as possible into the heads of the people who make choices — when alignment breaks, look for who owns the process before reaching for a new framework.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Cross-cutting thesis: process is an efficiency move for information to decision-makers; look for ownership before new frameworks.

## Why

Misalignment, informal workarounds, and abandoned initiatives usually trace to missing process ownership, not missing methodology.
