## Claim

You keep an experienced person on the side, at a distance — someone who has done this before — as a sounding board to phone and to coach a young lawyer on how to operate inside a company.

## Source quote or paraphrase

An experienced person on the side / at a distance is hugely valuable — sounding board, someone to phone, and to teach a young lawyer to operate in-house.

## Why

The intangible "how do I operate in-house" skill is where a junior lawyer most needs help, and an experienced outsider provides it without the cost of a senior hire.
