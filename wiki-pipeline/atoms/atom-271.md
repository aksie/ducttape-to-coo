## Claim

You know your reserved matters — the decisions that need approval from another body or third party (e.g. an investor) — and those approvals are documented when used.

## Source quote or paraphrase

The "decisions requiring approval from another body/third party" question is effectively the reserved matters question; use that term — it resonates.

## Why

Reserved matters are where investor consent rights bite; knowing and documenting them prevents acting without required approval and the disputes that follow.
