Watch for the classic pattern: the first in-house lawyer burns out within a year — they inherit years of accumulated mess, drown in day-to-day work, and never get to zoom out while big strategic risks go unaddressed.

## Source quote or paraphrase

The pattern to avoid: the first in-house lawyer burns out within a year — inherits years of mess, drowns in day-to-day, never zooms out; strategic risks go unaddressed.

## Why

A burned-out first hire is both a people failure and a risk failure — the strategic work they were hired for never happens.
