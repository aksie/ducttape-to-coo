## Claim

You should write a one-page communication norms doc (wiki or handbook) covering which tools/channels to use, expected response times, and what must be documented — and make it required reading for new hires.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Vohra: channel norms should be clearly written in a company wiki, required reading for new teammates, with meaningful updates pushed to the whole team.

## Why

Without written norms, each person picks channels randomly and response expectations drift — the team drowns in Slack while still missing decisions.
