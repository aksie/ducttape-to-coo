## Claim

HR or finance must receive the signed contract before the start date so payroll and insurance are active on day one.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner: contract was not submitted to HR/CFO/COO — person was not officially on payroll or insured until fixed.

## Why

A signed contract in a manager's inbox does not activate payroll or insurance. The handoff to HR is the operational definition of "hired."
