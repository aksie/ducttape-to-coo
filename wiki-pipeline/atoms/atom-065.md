## Claim
Multiple staff charging expenses on a shared company card with no per-person visibility leads to "nasty surprises at the end of the month."

## Source quote or paraphrase
Spendesk argues that as the team expands and salespeople or distributed staff begin charging on the company card, oversight breaks down without per-person controls.

## Why
A single shared card aggregates spending into one statement; there's no real-time signal about who spent what or whether it was authorized. By the time the statement arrives, the money is already gone and corrective conversations are awkward.
