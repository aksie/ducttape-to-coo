## Claim

You should treat Slack as a culture amplifier — without explicit norms it will entrench whatever response habits already exist, good or bad.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN: Slack left unchecked amplifies the worst parts of company culture; instant-response expectations come from the company, not the tool.

## Why

The tool defaults to synchronous urgency; unless you counter with written norms, fast-reply heroes become the implicit standard and deep work loses.
