## Claim
Confusing cash position with profitability is a common and dangerous mistake — deferred revenue and outstanding invoices make cash and profit diverge significantly.

## Source quote or paraphrase
CFO Dive sources note that founders frequently discover this distinction only when a cash crisis hits, by which point it's too late to act comfortably.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Cash in the bank includes customer prepayments you haven't earned yet (deferred revenue) and excludes invoices you've sent but not yet collected. A healthy cash balance can coexist with a loss-making P&L.
