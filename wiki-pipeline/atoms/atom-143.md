## Claim

Document your financial systems (one paragraph per tool) and your internal controls: who does what in finance, the four-eyes principle for payments above a threshold, and the authorised signatory list.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Document financial systems (one paragraph per tool). Document internal controls: who does what in finance, four-eyes principle (dual authorization for payments above threshold), authorized signatory list.

## Why

Internal controls documentation is a standard due diligence request. In its absence, buyers reconstruct it through interviews — a slow, error-prone process that extends the DD timeline. Having it written down also serves day-to-day operations: when a finance team member leaves, the process doesn't leave with them.
