## Claim
You can't quickly produce a receipt for any business expense the IRS might query about.

## Source quote or paraphrase
patio11 frames receipt collection as a goal: you should be organized well enough to find any specific receipt on demand for tax authorities.

## Why
Tax authorities can audit specific transactions years after the fact. If you can't produce evidence, the deduction is disallowed, costing you taxes plus penalties. The remediation cost when this hits is far higher than the prevention cost of organized receipt capture from day one.
