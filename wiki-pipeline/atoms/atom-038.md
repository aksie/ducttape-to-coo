## Claim
Dedicate the first all-hands of each quarter to reviewing last quarter's results and setting new goals together — make it a ritual, not a formality.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Davis: "Since OKRs launched, the first All Hands of every quarter has been devoted to talking about them." He uses this time to run through previous results, discuss why things went the way they did, and set direction for the next quarter.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Rituals create habits. When the all-hands has a consistent purpose — not just updates but goal accountability — it builds a culture where the team takes their goals seriously because they know they'll be asked about them.
