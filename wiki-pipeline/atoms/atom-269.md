## Claim

Material board decisions and the company's direction are documented (minutes, or at least an action list) — so in a "bad weather" scenario you can show you set course and acted in time.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Board decisions and direction are documented; in bad weather this is what a trustee looks back on — did you set course and act in time (e.g. closing a country in time, who gets paid, whether to keep hiring).

## Why

Without a written record of decisions and timing, you cannot demonstrate you acted responsibly when things went wrong — the documentation is the defence.
