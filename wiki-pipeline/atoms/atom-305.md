## Claim

Bringing legal fully in-house becomes a live question around Series A/B, when volume and strategic weight outgrow founders plus external counsel; before that point, episodic external help from a trusted generalist usually beats a full-time hire.

## Source quote or paraphrase

The first legal hire is typically a Series A/B question; before that, episodic external help (a trusted generalist) beats a full-time hire.

## Why

Hiring in-house too early creates the burnout trap with too little strategic work to justify it; episodic counsel matches cost to actual need until the volume is real.
