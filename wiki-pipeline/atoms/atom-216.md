## Claim

You should give each Slack channel a clear job — e.g. #general for company-wide, topic channels for work, separate space for social — and keep memes out of operational channels.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN: the "source of truth" and the place you post memes should not be the same place; Slack is not a good source of truth.

## Why

Mixing social and operational traffic trains everyone to monitor everything; separated channels make ignore-by-default safe.
