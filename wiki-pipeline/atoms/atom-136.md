## Claim

Maintain a compliance calendar — a simple spreadsheet or recurring calendar reminders — covering annual accounts deadline, tax filing dates, KvK filing, insurance renewals, trademark renewals, domain renewals, and WBSO application windows.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Compliance calendar (maintain as a simple spreadsheet or recurring calendar reminders): annual accounts filing deadline, corporate tax, KvK annual filing, insurance renewal dates, trademark renewal dates, domain renewal dates, WBSO application windows, works council consultation obligations if applicable.

## Why

Each of these deadlines carries a consequence for missing it — fines, lapsed protection, regulatory non-compliance. None of them send urgent reminders. A single place that lists what's due and when turns a reactive scramble into a routine check.
