## Claim

Only head of HR or CFO/COO sends the labour contract — from the lawyer-reviewed template, with documented signing authority — not a hiring manager's edited version.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Practitioner: CFO/COO or head of HR needs to send out the labour contract based on the agreed template; failure involved a self-edited contract and a signer without authority.

## Why

Template discipline and authorised signers keep IP clauses, NL employment terms, and compensation consistent. Manager-edited contracts bypass both legal and finance controls.
