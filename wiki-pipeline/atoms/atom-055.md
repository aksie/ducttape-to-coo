## Claim
Set up your expense categories correctly from day one — retroactive recategorisation is brutal and you will hate yourself later if you don't.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Multiple HN commenters emphasise that setting up proper expense categories from the start saves enormous pain later. Retroactive recategorisation is described as "brutal" by practitioners who have done it.

## Why
Your expense categories determine what you can see in your P&L and how you answer questions from investors, accountants, and yourself later. If categories are inconsistent or wrong from the start, every historical report is unreliable and you have to go line-by-line through months or years of transactions to fix it.
