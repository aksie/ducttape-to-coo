## Claim

Expect the first legal hire to start "3–0 down": founders built the company, hold strong views, and perceive legal as a blocker ("here comes the person who'll tell me it can't be done"), so the hire has to win trust back while cleaning up the backlog at the same time.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Founders see legal as a blocker; you start 3–0 down, have to win trust back, and clear the backlog at the same time.

## Why

If the founder relationship isn't actively managed, legal gets sidelined and the very risks it was hired to catch get overruled.
