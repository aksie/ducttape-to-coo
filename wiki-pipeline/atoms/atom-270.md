## Claim

Keep minutes of board/management meetings — an agenda with a couple of bullets per discussion point is enough — and consciously distinguish a real decision ("we are doing it") from something merely discussed ("a good idea").

## Source quote or paraphrase

Create minutes of board/management meetings (agenda + a few bullets per point is sufficient); separate a real board decision from a discussion.

## Why

Lightweight minutes make decisions traceable without bureaucracy; the hard part is naming what was actually decided versus just talked about.
