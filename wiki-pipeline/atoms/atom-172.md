## Claim

Everyone who joins the company — employee or contractor — receives something in writing immediately, at minimum a standard employment or contractor agreement.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Everyone who joins gets something in writing immediately. Not 'we are all friends, it will never go wrong.'

## Why

Written terms establish IP, confidentiality, and employment basics from day one; absence is a common and material finding in diligence years later.
