## Claim

The highest-value offboarding work is transferring tacit knowledge — how systems actually work, who to call, what is on fire — because that information only becomes visible when the person is gone.

## Source quote or paraphrase

HN commenter on Auth0 thread: in knowledge-work companies everyone carries unique information in their head (logins, backup schedules, pressing issues) that only surfaces once they leave.

## Why

Access revocation is table stakes; the operational black hole is what they knew but never wrote down. At 5 people one departure can stall a product line for weeks without structured transfer.
