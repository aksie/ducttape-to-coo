## Claim

You are behind if the team treats immediate Slack response as the default — that expectation is toxic to focus.

## Source quote or paraphrase

37signals: "Never expect or require someone to get back to you immediately unless it's a true emergency. The expectation of immediate response is toxic."

## Why

Instant-response culture forces constant context-switching; work that needs uninterrupted blocks (engineering, finance, hiring) stalls while chat stays busy.
