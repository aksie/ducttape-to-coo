## Claim

You are behind if you mandated "everything through Slack" and people are drowning — that policy confuses activity with progress.

## Source quote or paraphrase

Vohra: startups mandating 100% Slack sound intentional, but individuals are miserably drowning in an endless onslaught of comms.

## Why

Without channel jobs and permission to ignore untagged noise, Slack becomes a second inbox that interrupts deep work without improving alignment.
