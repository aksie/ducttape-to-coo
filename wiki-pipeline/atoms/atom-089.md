**Priorities are visible, not buried in a doc.** Use a whiteboard in the office, a pinned Slack message, or a single slide in the shared drive — the medium doesn't matter as long as it's impossible to miss. Put priorities where people already look: a pinned Slack message, office whiteboard, or Notion homepage. Pick one place and keep it current. If people have to hunt for priorities, they'll ignore them.
