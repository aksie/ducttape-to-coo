## Claim

As you grow past first hires, you should add lightweight automatic check-ins (daily what-you-did, weekly what-you'll-do) so transparency does not depend on more meetings.

## Source quote or paraphrase

37signals uses automatic daily and weekly check-ins shared company-wide, plus periodic heartbeats/kickoffs every ~6 weeks.

## Why

At early-revenue and beyond, ad-hoc updates stop scaling; predictable written check-ins replace status meetings without creating another chat firehose.
