## Claim
Document your expense policy and have every employee sign it — a verbal policy does not survive diligence and does not scale past 15 people.

## Source quote or paraphrase
Founderpath notes that verbal expense policies consistently fail diligence processes and that the absence of a signed expense policy is one of the top 5 administrative findings in Series A due diligence.

## Why (if explicit in source)
Verbal policies are interpreted differently by different people. A written, signed policy creates shared expectations and provides a reference point when disputes arise.
