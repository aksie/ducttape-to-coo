## Summary
Getexact argues that most startups wait too long to bring in financial leadership and outlines the progression from founder-led finances to fractional CFO to full-time hire. Covers when each transition makes sense and what to budget.

## Notable claims
- A fractional CFO becomes valuable around $1M ARR or Series A fundraising
- Full-time CFO typically justified at Series B or $10M+ ARR
- Cash flow modelling should be in place before any fundraising process begins
- Most early-stage founders underestimate how much time finance admin takes at 15-25 people
- Monthly close should be completed within 5 business days at this stage
