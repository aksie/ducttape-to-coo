## Summary
Warby Parker co-founder Dave Gilboa on how they built onboarding and employee experience from first hire onward. Strong practitioner voice. Directly relevant to First Hires phase as he reflects on what they did from employee #1.

## Notable claims
- Everything about onboarding is new to a new hire — everything makes an impact and sets the tone for how they perceive the culture
- Make new employees the most approachable people in the office, not the most invisible — design ice-breakers into the first days
- Senior leaders must personally deliver company values during onboarding — when founders articulate values, they carry far more weight than a poster or document
- A bad first week is hard to recover from; the impression formed in the first few days is sticky
- Onboarding should include something unexpected and culturally authentic — not just IT setup and a style guide
