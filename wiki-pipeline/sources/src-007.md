## Summary
Visible.vc (investor update software) outlines what financial metrics startups should be tracking and reporting at each stage. Obvious interest in promoting reporting cadence and tooling.

## Notable claims
- MRR, ARR, burn rate, and runway are the minimum financial metrics every investor-backed startup should report monthly
- Cash-on-hand and months of runway should be visible to the full leadership team, not just the CEO
- Board reporting should be on a fixed cadence with a consistent template — ad hoc updates erode trust
- Gross margin should be calculated and reported from the first month of revenue
- Net revenue retention is a leading indicator of financial health that belongs in every investor update
