# Pre-seed, seed startups with employees in office have higher revenue growth

HN thread discussing research showing pre-seed/seed startups with physical office have 3.5× higher revenue growth than fully remote. Key practitioner commentary:

- Pre-seed/seed stage is about finding PMF, which requires high iteration and fast collaboration — best done in the same room.
- Advantage diminishes as you grow past the size of one office room.
- The "radical colocation" effect (all in one room) is specifically what drives the advantage; different floors is roughly equivalent to remote in terms of collaboration quality.
- Coworking counted as "office" in the research — a dedicated company office is not required.
- Once past ~15-20 people and multiple rooms, the colocation advantage declines significantly.
