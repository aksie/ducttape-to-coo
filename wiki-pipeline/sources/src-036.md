## Summary

HR advisory overview of employee handbook requirements for Dutch companies. Covers the legally required sections (sick leave, working hours, discrimination policy) and typically recommended sections (expense policy, IT policy, code of conduct). Useful for understanding what the NL legal baseline actually requires in writing vs. what is discretionary.

## Notable claims

- Certain handbook sections are legally required in NL (sick leave procedure, working hours, non-discrimination)
- Others are strongly recommended but not legally mandated (expense policy, IT usage, social media)
- Dutch employment law places handbook obligations earlier (first hire) than many founders expect
- A written sick leave notification procedure is required by WVP
