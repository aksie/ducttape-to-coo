## Summary

HN thread on a Singapore contractor who deleted 180 VMs after early contract termination. Commenters diagnose broken offboarding: HR did not notify IT to shorten account expiry; contractor retained generic admin credentials from India. Used as cautionary tale for any team size — process failure, not only malice.

## Notable claims

- HR must create a ticket or notify IT when someone leaves early — do not rely on contract end date alone
- Revoke all credentials immediately on termination; generic shared admin accounts are a critical failure mode
- Individual accounts with elevated-privilege steps beat shared administrator logins
- Offboarding failure combines with poor access hygiene — both must be fixed
- Departed employee accessed systems weeks later using credentials that should have been invalidated on day one
