## Summary
Bench (bookkeeping service) covers the basics of startup bookkeeping — what to track, when to outsource, and common mistakes. Expected vendor interest in promoting outsourced bookkeeping.

## Notable claims
- Accrual basis accounting gives a more accurate picture of business health than cash basis once you have invoices outstanding
- Chart of accounts set up correctly from the start prevents painful restructuring later
- Separating COGS from operating expenses from day one makes gross margin visible without analytical overhead
- Monthly reconciliation of all accounts takes less than 2 hours with proper tooling
- The most common bookkeeping mistake is treating the bank balance as the P&L
