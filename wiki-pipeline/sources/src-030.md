# Verplichte RI&E beschermt je werknemers tegen gevaar — KVK

Official KVK (Dutch Chamber of Commerce) guidance on the mandatory RI&E (risico-inventarisatie en -evaluatie). Key legal requirements:

- **Every employer with at least one employee must have a RI&E** (risk inventory & evaluation) under Dutch Arbowet (Article 5).
- The RI&E must include: inventory of risks, evaluation of risks, a Plan van Aanpak (action plan), and regular updates.
- **Two exceptions where professional review is NOT required:**
  1. Employers with at most 40 hours/week of total employee hours (e.g., one 40-hour employee or two 20-hour employees).
  2. Employers with ≤25 employees who use a recognised branch-specific RI&E instrument.
- Failing to have a RI&E can result in a fine from the Dutch Labour Inspectorate (Nederlandse Arbeidsinspectie) and reduced insurance coverage.
- Each separate office location requires its own RI&E.
