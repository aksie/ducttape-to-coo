## Summary
patio11 (Patrick McKenzie) comments on Hacker News about practical expense management and bookkeeping for small companies and solo founders. Strong practitioner voice from running his own businesses. Covers card separation, receipt management, and when to engage an accountant.

## Notable claims
- Move all business expenses onto dedicated cards — no personal expense mixing
- Receipt retrieval should be instant for any transaction the IRS queries
- Engage an accountant early who can answer recurring cash-flow and tax questions
- At founder marginal tax rates (50%+), every missed deductible expense costs roughly half its value
