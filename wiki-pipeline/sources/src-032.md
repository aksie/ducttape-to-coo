# Employee Handbook Template for Startups — Promise Legal

Legal template vendor. Useful for understanding what a well-structured startup handbook covers regarding office/facilities and remote work. Key sections relevant to this cell:

- Section 11 (Remote Work and Hybrid Work): requires explicit policy decision — fully remote / hybrid / in-office with remote flexibility. Template has three options and customisation prompts.
- Section 15 (Health, Safety, and Workplace Accommodations): includes evacuation procedures, safety reporting, and accommodation process.
- The template explicitly warns: "Not updating the handbook to address remote work expectations" is a common mistake.
- For in-office setups: evacuation routes and assembly points should be posted; employees should know who to contact for safety issues.

Bias note: Promise Legal sells legal templates. Content is generic and designed to be customisable, not stage-specific. Useful as a reference for what categories need to be covered, not for practitioner insight on what actually matters at First Hires stage.
