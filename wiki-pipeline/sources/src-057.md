## Summary

Structured 25-step offboarding checklist aimed at small businesses. Chronological phases: day 1 acknowledge and assign transition owner; week 1 knowledge transfer and access audit; week 2 handoffs and exit interview; last day revoke access and recover devices. Vendor content but maps cleanly to first-hires scale when stripped of enterprise HR framing.

## Notable claims

- Day 1 of notice: acknowledge departure, notify stakeholders, set last day, assign transition owner
- Week 1: map responsibilities, schedule knowledge transfer sessions, document tribal knowledge, audit system access
- Exit interview in last week — after enough disengagement to be candid, before complete checkout
- Direct manager should not conduct exit interview alone; HR or skip-level leader gets more honest answers
- Last day: revoke all access, recover devices, forward email, process final pay, update records
