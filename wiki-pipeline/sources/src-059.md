## Summary

Synthesis of the repo investor-update template (anonymized portfolio examples + practitioner note). Not an external source — documents the recommended email structure for Phase 2 synthesis and wiki tools section.

## Notable claims

- Monthly short form: highlights, misses/help, lookback checkboxes, lookahead by area
- Quarterly adds: GTM metrics table, runway/burn, risk steering, scenario thinking
- Misses and help belong up front — investors trust updates that name problems early
- Lookback scores goals from quarterly goals doc or prior update with outcome explanations
- LLM-friendly: paste last update + goals + bullet dump to draft quickly; human reviews numbers
