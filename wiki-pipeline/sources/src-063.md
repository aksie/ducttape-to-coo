## Summary

Curated synthesis for process **1.4 Who Decides What @ first hires (2–10 people)**. Primary inputs: `source-discovery.md` (Tracks A–C), `docs/proposals/who-decides-what-summary-and-direction.md`, `blog/posts/drafts/who-decides-when-founders-disagree.md`, plus cross-links to 3.2 (hiring chain), 1.3 (codify decisions), 3.6 (founder sync @ foundation), 4.1/2.1 (written baseline / company runway).

**Pipeline decision:** Thin wiki from this source + super-atom `atom-306`. Tier 1 URLs (HN founder-mode, Shape Up, YC CEO essay, Grove/Advice process, Ascent ABC) cited through synthesis — not re-extracted as 20+ granular atoms unless a later pass needs them.

**Home message:** Founders align **with each other** first (domain defaults, runway context, founder sync); then publish one visible **approval list** for what still needs founder OK. Order matters — not the reverse.

## Two layers @ first hires

| Layer | What | Key sources |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Founder circle** | AOR (skill + passion), personal runway per founder, weekly founder sync | Track B (Lin, Buechler, YC Q7), B-fin (Munson, Mercury) |
| **Team-wide** | Approval list: hiring, spend >€X, pivot → founder OK; rest → owner with mandate | Track A (Shape Up, HN bottleneck, YC CEO), 3.2 |

## Schools (preserve in wiki)

| School | First hires position |
|--------|---------------------|
| Founder still decides most | Defensible at ~5 if documented |
| Delegate projects early | Shape Up / Fried — boundaries + team ownership |
| Single decider per decision | Light DRI in meetings, not a matrix |
| Consult → decide → commit | Advice process, Grove, DRI — input duty before close |
| Disagree and commit (label only) | Closing step after consult — not skip consultation |
| Passion partnership (Wistia) | Counter-school: need real buy-in in 2-founder creative pairs |
| **Middle (wiki default)** | Founder conversation → ABC one-pager → written approval list → practise letting go |

## ABC buckets (portable artefact)

- **A — Domain owner decides** after consulting affected people; partner commits once heard
- **B — Discuss, then tie-break** (CEO @ 2 founders) — pricing tests, mid-tier spend
- **C — Unanimous** — equity, fundraising, hire approval, large spend, pivot, sell

Team sees distilled Bucket C (+ escalate B to founders), not the whole map.

## Warning signs (extract)

Founder bottleneck on trivial OKs; team waits instead of acting; same fork argued for weeks; roles assumed from titles; personal runway never discussed; hire/spend outside approval list; decider skips consult → partner feels overruled.

## Evolution

Early-revenue: named owner per area + escalation thresholds; SPADE/RACI deferred to growth/scaled.
