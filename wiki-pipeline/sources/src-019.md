## Summary
Zeni (finance concierge service) argues for tracking expenses from day one, emphasising that retroactive categorisation becomes disproportionately harder as the business grows. Expected vendor interest in promoting outsourced finance operations.

## Notable claims
- Track expenses from day one, even if just a spreadsheet log
- Retrospectively organising expenses gets exponentially harder with time
- Memory of transaction context decays fast — current context is cheap to capture, expensive to reconstruct
