## Summary

Practitioner contribution on building and maintaining an HR filing structure. Key themes: every employment and freelance contract must be signed and centrally filed; IP assignment must be explicit and cannot be assumed from platform usage (Fiverr, Upwork, etc.); and the vertrouwenspersoon obligation is a legal requirement from first hire that many Dutch companies overlook.

## Notable claims

- Platform-based freelancers do not automatically confer IP ownership — a signed agreement with explicit assignment language is always required
- Missing IP assignment from early contractors is among the most common HR-related DD gaps
- Vertrouwenspersoon (confidential counsellor) is a legal obligation under the Arbowet from first employee, not just at 50+ headcount
- Transition from folder-based to HR system must designate a clear source of truth or you end up maintaining both in parallel
