## Summary
a16z outlines the stages of building a finance function from founder-led to CFO-led, with emphasis on what breaks at each transition. Heavy on Series A+ context.

## Notable claims
- Finance function typically lags 6-12 months behind where it needs to be in high-growth companies
- The first finance hire should own both FP&A and controllership — splitting too early creates coordination overhead
- Board reporting cadence should be established before Series A, not after
- Accrual accounting becomes necessary (vs. cash basis) once revenue recognition complexity increases
- Revenue recognition policy should be documented before any enterprise contracts are signed
