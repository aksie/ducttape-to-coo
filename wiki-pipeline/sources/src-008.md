## Summary
Founderpath covers the financial controls that need to be in place before a Series A diligence process. Useful for funded context; less relevant for bootstrapped companies at the same headcount.

## Notable claims
- Dual-signature approval for payments above a threshold (e.g. $5K) should be in place before Series A
- Expense policy must be documented and signed by all employees — verbal policies don't survive diligence
- Bank reconciliation should be completed monthly, not quarterly
- Credit card statements should be reconciled against receipts before month-end close
- Separation of duties between the person approving and the person paying reduces fraud risk significantly
