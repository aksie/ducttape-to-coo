## Cell-specific finding

**This is a sparse cell.** At 2–10 people (First Hires stage), "Office & Facilities" barely exists as a distinct operational process. The main concerns are:

1. **Structural decision**: remote vs. office — needs to be made explicitly, not defaulted to
2. **Ownership assignment**: if you have office space, one named person must be responsible for it
3. **Minimal compliance**: in NL, RI&E is legally mandatory from the first employee even for a small office
4. **Not locking in commitments**: coworking/flex is default-good; long leases at this stage are a common expensive mistake

The absence of rich practitioner content on "facilities management at 2–10 people" is itself a finding: the process barely exists. What exists is essentially one sentence: agree on who is responsible, and cover the legal minimum.
