## Notable findings

- GitLab's company-level planning appears to evolve from shared strategy context and prioritization norms, not from a formal planning process.
- The 2016 strategy page is the key early-stage artifact: mission, goals, sequence, constraints, assumptions, and functional goals appear before OKRs.
- OKRs show up as a scaling mechanism once organizational layers and functional owners need to connect their work to company goals.
- The cadence pages show the late-stage shift: planning becomes an operating rhythm, then a hierarchy, then a maintained system with review cadences.
