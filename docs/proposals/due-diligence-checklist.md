# DUE DILIGENCE CHECKLIST

---

## 1. FINANCIAL DUE DILIGENCE

### Financial Statements & Reporting

Prior years revenue, costs & P&L — summarised in a presentation with supporting documentation organised as follows:

- **Formal documentation**
  - Audited financial statements (last 3 years) — all PDFs (annual accounts signed off by the General Meeting of Shareholders)
  - Tax filings (last 5 years), including R&D tax credits and incentives, tax contingencies and disputes, sales tax / VAT compliance across jurisdictions — all PDFs

- **Bridge from formal to management financials**
  - Explanation of revenue recognition policies (especially for subscription, advertising, licensing) and EBITDA reconciliation and adjustments — one-pager (differences between statutory reporting and operational practice: how the numbers are built up and why figures differ)
  - Bridge from statutory financials to management financials — one-pager or Excel with management financials

- **Management reporting** (current year / last 18 months, links to earlier periods where available)
  - In whatever format is available — Excel or Google Sheets (all monthly management reports)

- **Current status**
  - P&L: unaudited interim financials (current year) — Excel or Google Sheets
  - Balance sheet:
    - Working capital and receivables / payables — ability to take a spot check of each; link to bank login; links to contracts for receivables / payables where applicable
    - Debt & liabilities — list with 5-line summary and links to documentation
  - Forecast, sensitivities & key risks — one-pager maximum

---

### Revenue Breakdown

- Revenue breakdown by stream (subscriptions, advertising, content licensing, syndication)
  - In management Excel / Google Sheets (high-level chart, 3 years back)
- Churn rates and customer lifetime value (CLV), cohort analysis by customer acquisition period (churn, CAC and LTV), geographic revenue segmentation (NL vs. EU)
  - In management Excel / Google Sheets or a separate deeper analysis file
- Revenue growth trends and seasonality
  - Same format, 3 years back
- Pricing models and changes over time
  - Timeline in Excel / Google Sheets, 3 years back
- Business model risks — one-pager
  - Subscriber acquisition cost trends
  - Churn rate acceleration
  - Other key risks

---

### Cost Breakdown

- Operating expense analysis by category, historical
  - Charts in management reporting Excel / Sheets

---

### Financial Systems

- ERP and financial software (e.g. Exact)
  - One paragraph per tool; billing and collection systems — list with one-line description (Exact, Zenvoices, MOSS, payroll systems, HR — Human Wave, etc.)
- Internal controls assessment
  - Link to process description or a quick-and-dirty process description: division of finance responsibilities, four-eyes principle (dual authorisation), authorised signatory list
- Revenue tracking and analytics tools
  - List with one-line description (e.g. Stripe, Google Analytics)

---

## 2. LEGAL DUE DILIGENCE

### Corporate Documents

- Core corporate documents — all PDFs: deed of incorporation, shareholder register, cap table and ownership structure, entity chart (subsidiaries, joint ventures), General Meeting of Shareholders resolutions, minutes, articles of association, editorial charter, shareholder register BV, etc.
- Cap table history / timeline — Google Sheets or Excel

---

### Material Contracts

- Material contracts — start with the top 10 customers and suppliers; further detail depending on size per customer / supplier and percentage of costs / revenue
  - List in Excel / Google Sheets with one-line description and link to PDF of contract

---

### Litigation & Disputes

- Litigation history — all legal proceedings and rulings
  - Overview in chronological timeline with specifics
  - All PDFs (last 5 years)

---

### Intellectual Property

- Key IP overview: content ownership documentation, copyright registrations and assignments, licensing overview
- IP assignment agreements from employees and contractors
  - Links to signed PDFs

---

### Insurance

- Insurance policies — list with one-line description per policy
  - Link to PDF of each contract

---

## 3. HUMAN RESOURCES DUE DILIGENCE

### Organisation & Personnel

- Org chart + headcount — one slide
- Key personnel and retention plans (bonuses and succession plans)
  - Mark on org chart: red / orange / green for ease of replacement
- Compensation summary (salary + bonus)
  - List in Excel / Google Sheets
- Disputes and settlement agreements
  - List in Excel / Google Sheets with links to underlying PDFs

---

### Metrics

- Core HR metrics: turnover, attrition, absenteeism %, employee satisfaction surveys
  - In whatever format is available; simple Excel / Google Sheets is fine

---

### Employment Agreements (current)

- Employee contracts — start with key employees
  - Link from org chart to contract directory (PDFs or HR system)
- Standard offer letter templates
  - Google Docs / PDF / Word
- Additional appendices: non-compete and non-solicitation agreements, confidentiality and IP assignment agreements
  - Google Docs / PDF / Word; link to contract directory
- Freelance agreements and IP assignment, including standard template
  - Link to contract directory (PDFs or HR system)

---

### Labour Relations

- Collective labour agreements and union contracts, including explanation of steps taken and timeline
  - All PDFs
- Labour disputes
  - All PDFs
- Works council / confidential advisory committee
  - Summary of meetings; link to directory of PDFs (last year)
  - Overview of how the confidential advisory committee is set up (including external confidential counsellor, regular meetings, confirmation of no current or recent situations)

---

### Training & Development *(optional)*

- Onboarding programmes
- Training budget and utilisation

---

## 4. TECHNOLOGY & OPERATIONS DUE DILIGENCE

### Technology Stack

- System architecture diagram (including cloud infrastructure, database architecture and analytics platforms) — one-page PDF
- Web and technology stack — one-pager
- Technology inventory (languages, frameworks, platforms) — list
- Third-party dependencies, SLAs and APIs; open source software inventory — one-pager or a few paragraphs
- Documentation quality — assessment by key technical staff; link to code
- Code repository access (GitHub / GitLab)

---

### Security & Compliance

- Security architecture and controls (including DDoS protection) — one-page PDF
- Authentication and authorisation systems — one-page PDF
- Penetration testing results — link to directory of PDFs
- Data backup and disaster recovery — one-page PDF or available process description
- Incident response plan — one-page PDF or available process description

---

### Product & Development

- Product roadmap (12–24 months) — 5 slides or one page

---

### Additional depth *(usually not required at standard DD stage)*

- Code review with technical advisors
- Code coverage and automated testing
- Static code analysis results
- Technical debt assessment

---

## 5. COMMERCIAL & MARKET DUE DILIGENCE

### Market Position *(3–6 slides)*

- Competitive landscape analysis
- Unique value proposition
- Competitive advantages and moats
- Channel mix, historical (Excel / Google Sheets)
